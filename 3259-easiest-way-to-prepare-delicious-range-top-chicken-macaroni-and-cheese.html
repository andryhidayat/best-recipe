Ingredients of Range-Top Chicken, Macaroni, and Cheese

  1. Prepare 1 1/2 cup of dried multigrain or regular elbow macaroni.
  2. It's 12 oz of skinless, boneless chicken breast halves, cut into 1 inch pieces.
  3. It's 1/4 cup of finely chopped onion.
  4. It's 6 1/2 oz of cream cheese ( garden and herd flavored).
  5. You need 1 2/3 cup of fat-free milk.
  6. You need 1 tbsp of all-purpose flour.
  7. It's 3/4 cup of shredded reduced-fat cheddar cheese.
  8. You need 2 cup of baby spinach (fresh).
  9. Prepare 1 cup of cherry tomatoes, cut in half.

Range-Top Chicken, Macaroni, and Cheese step by step

  1. In a medium saucepan cook macaroni according to package directions, except dont add any salt to water. (Unless you want to) drain..
  2. Meanwhile, coat a large nonstick skillet with cooking spray; heat skillet over medium-high heat. Add chicken and onion to hot skillet. Cook for 4 to 6 minutes or until chicken is no longer pink..
  3. Add cream cheese; stir until melted..
  4. In a medium bowl whisk together milk and flour. Add all at once to chicken mixture. Cook and stir until thickened and bubbly..
  5. Reduce heat to low. Add cheddar cheese, stirring until melted..
  6. Add cooked macaroni; cook and stir foe 1 to 2 minutes until heated through..
  7. Stir in spinach. Top with cherry tomatoes..
  8. Makes 5 (1 1/3-cup) servings..
