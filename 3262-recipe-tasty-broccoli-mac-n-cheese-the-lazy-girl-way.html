Broccoli Mac N Cheese the lazy girl way! step by step

  1. bring 6 cups of water to boil.
  2. once at a boil add broccoli let it cook for 3-5 mins depending on how big your broccoli is..
  3. remove powder packet from box of Mac n cheese and add noodles to the broccoli which is cooking in boiling water.
  4. here is where it gets really hard people (just kidding)... follow the Mac n cheese cooking directions on the back of the box..
  5. once noodles are tender. strain them and return to the same pan you boiled them in..
  6. add powder cheese packet found in the Mac in cheese box, add milk according to your liking or according to the back of the box. o didn't add butter to this recipe, but you can if you want..
  7. slice 2 pieces of Velveeta, add as much or as little to taste..
  8. cube the Velveeta cheese for easy melting..
  9. add cubed Velveeta to the Mac n cheese. stir over low heat until cheese is melted. here u can add more butter or milk to get the consistency you desire..
