All in one party salad step by step

  1. For fruit tree : cut all fruits in any shapes. Stick in carrot with help of toothpick..
  2. For vegetables salad: mix all grated vegetables like carrot, cabbage and beet add salt and black pepper as per taste. Mix it very well sprinkle coriander leaves..
  3. For corn salad: add boiled corn in bowl add salt and cheese and mix it well put into half capsicum..
  4. For tangy salad: add raw mango diced and cucumber diced and salt in bowl and mix it. Put it into half capsicum..
  5. For sprouts: mix chana and moong add salt in it put into capsicum..
  6. Garnishing:- take two strawberry and banana make santa clause from it. From carrot make deer of santa. From cucumber make car of santa..
  7. Assemble:- first take one squre shape tray. One corner of tray put Christmas fruit tree. Than put dil leaves in middle of tray arrange santa cart and deer on it. Other side of corner put three half capsicum and in middle of three capsicum put vegetables salad..
