Ingredients of Macaroni and cheese muffins

  1. Prepare 2 cup of whole grain macaroni.
  2. It's 1 cup of shredded cheddar cheese.
  3. You need 2 tbsp of butter.
  4. It's 2 tbsp of whole wheat flour.
  5. Prepare 2 cup of milk.
  6. You need of salt.
  7. You need of pepper.
  8. It's 1 cup of broccoli florets, uncooked.
  9. Prepare 1/2 cup of frozen peas.
  10. You need 1/2 tsp of paprika.

Macaroni and cheese muffins instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 and grease muffin pan or add cupcake liners.
  2. Boil macaroni according to package directions.
  3. In a skillet, melt butter and add flour. Whisk together over medium heat for a few minutes.
  4. Gradually add milk to butter and flour. Whisk constantly for 10 minutes over medium heat until it begins to thicken.
  5. Remove skillet from heat and stir in most of cheese and all of the macaroni.
  6. Salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Fill muffin cups with 2 tablespoons of mac and cheese mixture.
  8. Add a layer of broccoli or peas to each cup.
  9. On top of the vegetables, fill each muffin cup with remaining mac and cheese mixture.
  10. Sprinkle paprika and cheese on top of each muffin.
  11. Bake 20-30 minutes, until muffins begin to brown.
