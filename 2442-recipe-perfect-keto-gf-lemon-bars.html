Ingredients of Keto GF Lemon Bars

  1. It's of Crust.
  2. It's 2 sticks of butter (softened).
  3. You need 2 cups of coconut flour.
  4. Prepare of Zest from 6-8 lemons.
  5. You need 1 tsp of vanilla extract.
  6. Prepare 1/3 cup of Stevia sweetener.
  7. Prepare 1 of egg (2 if you want the crust less crumbly).
  8. Prepare 1/4 tsp of salt.
  9. You need of Topping.

  11. It's of The pulp and juice of all lemons (2 cups+).
  12. It's 1 tsp of baking soda.
  13. It's 8 of eggs.
  14. Prepare 3 scoops of beef gelatin (optional).

Keto GF Lemon Bars step by step

  1. Mix all crust ingredients and press into bottom of 2 greased baking dishes. Cook at 315 for 25min..
  2. Mix all topping ingredients and pour on top of baked crust. Bake on 350 for 25min. When you pull the lemon bars out, then sprinkle powdered stevia and place lemon bars in the fridge to cool..
