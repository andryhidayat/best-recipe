Potato Stuffed Semolina Sandwitch instructions

  2. First cook potato by 3 whistle in cooker. take one kadai add one spoon oil heat it add cuted onion tomato green chilly turmeric powder and salt as per taste into it. after 2 min add mashed potato and lemon it and mix it..
