Ingredients of Classic Macaroni Salad from Muller's

  1. You need of salad ingredients.
  2. It's 3/4 cup of mayonnaise.
  3. You need 2 tbsp of vinegar.
  4. Prepare 1 tbsp of prepared mustard.
  5. Prepare 1 tsp of sugar.
  6. It's 1 tsp of salt.
  7. It's 1 dash of pepper.
  8. You need 1 pinch of celery seed.
  9. You need 8 oz of elbow macaroni.
  10. It's 1/2 cup of sliced celery.
  11. It's 1 cup of chopped green or red pepper.
  12. Prepare 1/4 cup of chopped onion.

Classic Macaroni Salad from Muller's step by step

  1. In large bowl stir together mayo, vinegar, mustard, sugar, salt, pepper and celery seed. Stir until smooth..
  2. Boil macaroni until tender, drain and rinse in cold water..
  3. Add all ingredients together, toss to cost well..
  4. Cover, chill..
  5. Makes five cups. An old recipe from Mueller's..
