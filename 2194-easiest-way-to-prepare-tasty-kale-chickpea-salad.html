Ingredients of Kale Chickpea salad

  1. Prepare 1 of lemon juice.
  2. You need 1 clove of garlic chopped.
  3. It's of salt and pepper.
  4. Prepare 2 tbsp of olive oil.
  5. Prepare 1 cup of kale.
  6. Prepare 1/2 cup of boiled chick peas.
  7. Prepare 1/2 of ripe avocado chopped.
  8. You need 1/4 of yellow bell pepper chopped.
  9. Prepare 1/4 of orange bell pepper chopped.
  10. It's 1 of carrot chopped.
  11. Prepare 1/2 of beetroot chopped.
  12. Prepare 1 of onion chopped.
  13. You need of pumpkin seeds.
  14. You need of sunflower seeds.
  15. Prepare of sesame seeds.
  16. Prepare of himalayan rock salt.

Kale Chickpea salad step by step

  1. Chop and massage Kale with olive oil,salt and a chopped garlic clove.
  2. Add boiled chickpeas,kale,yellow bell pepper,orange bell pepper,carrot,red onion,beetroot,avocado in a bowl,.
  3. Prepare lemon dressing for the salad by adding lemon juice, extra virgin olive oil, salt, black pepper, Himalayan black salt and add to the bowl..
  4. Also sprinkle sesame,sunflower and pumpkin seeds for extra nutrients.
