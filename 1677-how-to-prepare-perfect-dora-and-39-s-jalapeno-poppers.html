Ingredients of Dora's Jalapeño Poppers

  1. It's 3 large of Jalapeños.
  2. Prepare 8 oz of cream cheese.
  3. Prepare 1 cup of Mexican blend cheese (more or less to liking).
  4. Prepare 1 pinch of salt.
  5. It's 1 cup of flour.
  6. Prepare 1 cup of milk.
  7. Prepare 1 cup of bread crumbs.

Dora's Jalapeño Poppers instructions

  1. Put gloves on!! Make a t shaped cut into jalapeño and seed completely. Be careful for it not to fall apart.
  2. Mix cream cheese, Mexican blend, salt.
  3. Stuff jalapeños with mixture.
  4. Dip jalapeños in milk and roll in the flour, let sit for 5-10 min to dry..
  5. Dip in milk again and roll on bread crumbs. Repeat this step if you want them extra crunchy.
  6. Spray a pan with non stick spray place jalapeños in a row..
  7. Add to oven on 350 for about an hour or till visabley brown.
  8. Enjoy!!.
