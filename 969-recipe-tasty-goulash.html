Ingredients of Goulash

  1. You need 1 lb of ground turkey.
  2. You need 2 c of elbow pasta.
  3. Prepare 1 of onion, chopped.
  4. You need 2 cloves of garlic, minced.
  5. You need 28 oz of diced tomatoes.
  6. It's 3/4 c of carrots, chopped.
  7. Prepare 1 tsp of kosher salt.
  8. It's 1/2 tsp of black pepper.
  9. It's 2 tsp of Italian seasoning.
  10. It's 2 c of water.

Goulash step by step

  1. Add garlic and turkey and cook through..
  2. Add carrots, seasonings, and tomatoes and simmer 5 minutes..
  3. Add water and bring pot back to a simmer..
  4. Add pasta and cook according to package directions..
  5. Add extra salt to taste. Top with parmesan cheese..
