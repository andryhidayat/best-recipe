Ingredients of Game day bites

  1. It's 1 packages of johnsonville weenie dogs.
  2. Prepare 2 packages of of refrigerated homestyle biscuits.
  3. It's 10 slice of sharp cheddar cheese.
  4. It's 1 of deli mustard (for dipping).

Game day bites instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 400°.
  2. Flatten out each biscuit into a long thin strip. Fold each piece of cheese in have and separate to have a total of 20 small slices.
  3. Place biscuit strip on a nonstick pan, place a slice of cheese and a dog on the biscuit..
  4. Roll up each biscuit an place end side down, bake in preheated oven for 13 minutes or until biscuits are light brown. Serve with mustard and enjoy!.
