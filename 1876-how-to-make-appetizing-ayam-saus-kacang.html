Ingredients of Ayam saus kacang

  1. Prepare 125 g of roasted unsalted peanuts.
  2. You need 2 of onions, roughly chopped.
  3. You need 3 of garlic cloves.
  4. You need 1/4 cup (60 ml) of sweet soy sauce*.
  5. You need 2 teaspoons of brown sugar.
  6. It's 2 teaspoons of chilli sauce (not sweet chilli).
  7. You need 300 ml of coconut milk.
  8. You need 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil.
  9. You need 4 of chicken breasts on the bone, cut into 3 pieces with a cleaver or sharp knife.
  10. Prepare 275 ml of Massel chicken style liquid stock.
  11. You need 1 of long red chilli, seeds removed, very thinly sliced, to garnish.
  12. It's 2 of kaffir lime leaves, finely shredded, to garnish.
  13. You need of Steamed jasmine rice, to serve.

Ayam saus kacang instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 170Â°C. Pulse nuts, onion and garlic in a food processor to a rough paste. Add ketjap, sugar, chilli sauce and 1 tablespoon coconut milk. Set aside..
  2. Heat oil in a large frypan over medium heat and brown chicken all over. Place on a baking tray and transfer to the oven while you make the sauce..
  3. Drain fat from pan. Add peanut paste and cook 1-2 minutes until fragrant..
  4. Add stock and remaining coconut milk, bring to boil, then simmer for 5 minutes. Return chicken to pan and cook for a further 5 minutes.
