Hydrabadi chicken dum biryani instructions

  1. Chicken marination - for at least 2 hrs or good if kept over night..
  2. Take curd, add haldi red chilli powder, salt, green chilli, whole garam masala,add this to the chicken and lemon also keep for marination..
  3. Soak rice for at least 1/2 and hr..
  4. Boil water for the rice by adding salt, whole garam masala,and bring it Boil then add the rice and cook nicely cook untill 60 %. And drain it..
  5. Now put layers of chicken marinated above it put the boil rice and layer of mint and coriander and fried onions and a cup of oil. And saffron colour.
  6. Keep it on dum by covering properly so that the steam don't escape out. Cook on med high flame for 10 min then on low med flame to 20 to 25 min..
