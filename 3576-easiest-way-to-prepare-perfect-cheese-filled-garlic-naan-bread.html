Ingredients of Cheese Filled Garlic Naan Bread

  1. Prepare 2 tsp of sugar.
  2. You need 1 tbs of yeast.
  3. It's 4 cups of flour.
  4. It's 1/2 cup of yogurt.
  5. You need 1/4 cup of ghee.
  6. You need 1 tsp of salt.
  7. Prepare 2 cups of shredded mozzarella cheese.
  8. Prepare 10 of finely chopped garlic cloves.
  9. Prepare 2 tbsp of butter melted.
  10. It's 1 1/3 cup of warm milk.
  11. Prepare of Cilantro chopped.

Cheese Filled Garlic Naan Bread instructions

  1. In a bowl combine milk and sugar. Add yeast and let it sit for 15 minutes..
  2. Add flour, yogurt, ghee and salt. With the help of spatula mix until flour is well incorporated..
  3. Knead the dough. And cover the dough with a clean cloth and keep it for about 1 -2 hours in warm place and let the dough rise..
  4. Take the dough and punch the air out. Place it on floured surface. Knead it And make small balls (Pairay) of it..
  5. Take the ball and knead it and flatten it with the help of rolling pin and put 3-4 tbsps of cheese in the centre..
  6. Pull the edges and seal the dough. Bring it back to ball shape. And flatten it again with the help of rolling pin..
  7. Sprinkle some garlic on the dough and with the help of rolling pin gently press the garlic. With a brush, spread butter on the dough..
  8. Place the dough with garlic and butter carefully on the pan / tawa. And cook it on low flame and let the cheese inside melt. You can decide it's thickness while rolling the dough..
