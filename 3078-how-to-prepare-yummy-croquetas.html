Ingredients of Croquetas

  1. Prepare 200 grams of chicken leftovers.
  2. It's 150 grams of jamón serrano.
  3. It's 1 of boiled egg (optional).
  4. Prepare 1 tbsp of butter.
  5. You need 1 pinch of salt.
  6. You need 1 pinch of pepper.
  7. Prepare 1 pinch of numteg.
  8. Prepare 1 of egg (beated).
  9. Prepare 2 tbsp of breadcrumbs.
  10. You need 2 cup of milk ( you may need more depending on how thick you like the bechamel).
  11. Prepare 3 tbsp of all purpose flour.
  12. It's 1 of olive oil.

Croquetas instructions

  1. chop the chicken, jamón and the boiled egg if used and mix them all.
  2. in a sauce pan, melt the butter add half of the flour and some warm milk. Stirr constantly while adding the rest of the flour and milk until it thikens..
  3. Mix the bechamel you just made with the chicken-jamón mixture. Let it cool at least for 2h.
  4. Using two spoons, make croquetas with the mixture..
  5. Deep them into the egg and then into the breadcrumbs..
  6. Fry them in olive oil. Once they are golden, let them cool in absorvent paper and serve..
