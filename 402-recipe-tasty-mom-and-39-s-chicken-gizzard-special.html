Ingredients of Mom's chicken gizzard special

  1. Prepare 100 gr of shrimp shelled and cut open the back to clean the dirt.
  2. Prepare 100 gr of jelly mushroom.
  3. It's Half of a pineapple thinly cut.
  4. Prepare of Ginger matchstick cut.
  5. Prepare of Garlic 2 cloves bruised.
  6. Prepare 100 gr of chicken gizzard sliced into small bits.
  7. It's of Salt and pepper.
  8. You need 1 table spoon of Rice wine vinegar.
  9. Prepare of Oil.
  10. You need of Water.
  11. You need of Cornstarch.

Mom's chicken gizzard special step by step
