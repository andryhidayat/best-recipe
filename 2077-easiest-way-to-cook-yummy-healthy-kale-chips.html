Healthy Kale Chips.

You can cook Healthy Kale Chips using 5 ingredients and 6 steps. Here is how you achieve that.

Ingredients of Healthy Kale Chips

  1. Prepare 1 bunch of kale.
  2. You need of Olive oil or truffle oil.
  3. It's of Salt.
  4. Prepare of Pepper.
  5. Prepare of Optional: garlic powder and lime.

Healthy Kale Chips instructions

  1. Wash your bunch of kale, let it dry, it must be very dry.
  2. Cut the large stem from the bottoms of the leaves, cut the kale leaves to prices.
  3. Heat oven to 350°.
  4. Place the dry kale leaves in a baking sheet add oil to the leaves and salt and pepper, using your hands toss the kale leaves (add garlic powder if you want).
  5. Bake for 10 to 12 minutes or until the kale leaves are crunchy.
  6. Place the crispy kale chips in a bowl and toss once more, add a squirt of lime for a nice tangy flavor and enjoy.
