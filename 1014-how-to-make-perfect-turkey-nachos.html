Turkey nachos instructions

  1. Brown ground turkey the add taco seasoning...do not drain liquid or add more liquid....flavor is keeped together.
  2. Dice up all ingredients....I don't like chunky nachos but you may cut them the way you like best!.
  3. 9x13 glass baking pan layer tostito scoops on bottom & make sure turned up so each scoop can be filled.
  4. Gradually fill with each ingredient with cheese on top.
  5. Add new layer of nachos & duplicate....should get at least 3 layers in one pan.
  6. Preheat oven to 375' and bake until cheese is melted or your nose can smell the aroma.
