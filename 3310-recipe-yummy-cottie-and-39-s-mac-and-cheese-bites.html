Ingredients of Cottie's mac and cheese bites

  1. It's 1/2 box of Elbow macaroni.
  2. Prepare 1 1/2 tbsp of Butter.
  3. You need 1/4 cup of Parmesan cheese.
  4. Prepare 2 tbsp of Flour.
  5. You need 3/4 cup of Milk.
  6. It's 1 cup of Cheddar cheese.
  7. Prepare 1 cup of American cheese.
  8. Prepare 1/4 tsp of Paprika.
  9. You need 2 large of Egg yolks.

Cottie's mac and cheese bites step by step

  1. Preheat 425 oven.
  2. Cook macaroni as per directions.
  3. Brush non stick mini muffin pans with butter, 48 total. Sprinkle with parmesan.
  4. Melt butter in sauce pan..
  5. Whisk in flour for 2 minutes, medium heat.
  6. Whisk in milk until boiling, about 5 minutes, keep stirring.
  7. Add cheeses, whisk until melted.
  8. Remove from heat and fold in the egg yolk and paprika.
  9. At this point I might add some salt, but I am not sure it is necessary.
  10. Spoon into muffin pan, sprinkle with parmesan.
  11. Bake for 10 minutes.
  12. Let cool at least 5 minutes.
  13. Transfer to platter and serve.
