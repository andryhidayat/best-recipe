Ingredients of easy sloppy joe casserole

  1. You need 6 large of hashbrowns.
  2. Prepare 1 lb of ground meat.
  3. You need 1 can of sloppy joe sauce.
  4. It's 1/2 cup of cheese.
  5. You need 1/4 cup of frozen spinach.

easy sloppy joe casserole instructions

  1. brown meat till done and drain.
  2. reuturn to pan add spinach and sauce and stir on low till boiling.
  3. fry up hashbrowns till done.
  4. preheat oven to 350 and take a bread pan add three hashbrowns to bottom.
  5. add half sloppy joe sauce on top.
  6. then add three more hashbrowns.
  7. add cheese then rest of sloppy joe sauce on top.
  8. bake for 10 min on 350.
