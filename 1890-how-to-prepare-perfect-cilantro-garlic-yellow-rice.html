Ingredients of Cilantro Garlic Yellow Rice

  1. You need 1/2 of a medium onion, diced.
  2. It's 3 Tablespoons of minced garlic (about 5 large cloves).
  3. Prepare 1/4 cup of chopped cilantro (you can use the stems, too).
  4. You need 1.5 Tablespoons of butter (I use unsalted.).
  5. Prepare 1 teaspoon of ground achiote (aka) annatto.
  6. Prepare 2 cups of jasmine rice.
  7. You need 2 cups of unsalted chicken or vegetable stock.
  8. Prepare 1 cup of water.
  9. You need 3/4-1 teaspoon of kosher salt.
