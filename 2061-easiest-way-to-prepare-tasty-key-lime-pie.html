Ingredients of Key Lime Pie

  2. You need 1 of , 9" ready made frozen pie crust. You may make your own but ready made gets this on the table quicker.
  3. Prepare 14 oz of can of sweetened condensed milk.
  4. It's 3 of egg yolks plus one whole egg.
  5. It's 1/2 cup of key lime juice, about 16-20 key limes.
  6. You need 2 tsp of key lime zest.
  7. Prepare 2 tbsp of sugar.
