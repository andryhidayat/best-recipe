Ingredients of Macaroni Salad with Veggies

  1. Prepare 1/2 cup of fat-free mayonnaise.
  2. You need 1/8 cup of white vinegar.
  3. Prepare 1 tbsp of dijon mustard.
  4. Prepare 1 tsp of salt.
  5. Prepare 1/2 tsp of pepper.
  6. You need 1/2 tsp of cayenne pepper.
  7. You need 1/2 tbsp of sugar.
  8. It's 1/2 cup of red bell pepper, diced.
  9. Prepare 1/2 cup of yellow or orange bell pepper, diced.
  10. Prepare 1/2 cup of green onions, diced.
  11. It's 1/2 cup of carrots, shredded.
  12. You need 1 cup of sugar snap peas, blanched.
  13. It's 1/4 cup of jalapeno pepper, diced.
  14. You need 1/4 cup of poblano pepper, diced.
  15. You need 8 oz of uncooked pasta.

Macaroni Salad with Veggies step by step

  1. Mix mayonnaise, vinegar, mustard, salt, pepper, and cayenne pepper together. Whisk in sugar..
  2. Stir in chopped veggies - bell peppers, green onions, carrots, peas, jalapeno, and poblano pepper. Refrigerate..
  3. Cook pasta according to instructions, drain without rinsing. Allow pasta to slowly come to room temperature..
  4. Mix pasta and dressing in a large bowl until pasta is well coated. Refrigerate overnight..
