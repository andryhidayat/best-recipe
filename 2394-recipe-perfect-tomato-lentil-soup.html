Ingredients of Tomato lentil soup

  1. It's 2 tbsp of mixed dal.
  2. You need 3-4 pc of tomato roughly chopped.
  3. Prepare 3 of garlic cloves.
  4. It's 1 of small piece ginger (1/2 tsp finely chopped).
  5. You need 1 of small onion diced salt as required.
  6. It's 1 of small onion finely chopped.
  7. You need 2-3 tbsp of capsicum finely chopped.
  8. It's Pinch of black pepper powder(less then 1/4tsp).
  9. You need Pinch of cinnamon powder (1inch cinnamon stick).
  10. You need 1 tsp of Jaggery (optional).

Tomato lentil soup step by step

  1. Wash and soak dal in hot water for 30min. Then add chopped tomatoes, ginger, garlic, onion and salt as require. Add enough water..
  2. Give 3 whistle and let it cool down little. Then churn with hand blender..
  3. Now, add finely chopped onion and capsicum..
  4. Now add black pepper powder and cinnamon powder. (If u don't have cinnamon powder, then add cinnamon stick while cooking daal and tomato).
  5. Simmer soup for 2-3 mins. Then transfer to a bowl and serve hot with salad or khakhara Or any fried items..
