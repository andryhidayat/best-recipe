Lemon Dijon Chicken, potatoes and green beans step by step

  1. Season chicken with salt and pepper while you prep your veg.
  2. Heat oil in cast iron or oven proof skillet, sear/brown chicken on both sides. Remove chicken and set aside..
  3. Add the potatoes, garlic, seasoning, and dijon to the pan and mix everything together in the remaining oil..
