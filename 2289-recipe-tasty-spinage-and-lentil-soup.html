Spinage and Lentil Soup step by step

  1. Cook lentils with water on high for 20 minutes stirring to keep from sticking..
  2. Turn down stove to medium low and cook 20 more minutes stirring occasionally..
  3. While lentils cook, chop up your vegetables and add to lentils as you finish chopping each one..
  4. Add the rest of the ingredients to lentils and turn the stove up to medium high and cook until vegetables are cooked thru..
  5. Soup will be hot so be careful when serving. Enjoy!.
