White Rice and Finadene instructions

  1. Heat up white rice.
  2. Chop onions.
  3. Add tomatoes.
  4. Squeeze lime.
  5. Add 2 tablespoons of water.
  6. Add jalapenos.
  7. Add salt and pepper to taste.
  8. Muddle/mix.
  9. Add to rice and serve.
