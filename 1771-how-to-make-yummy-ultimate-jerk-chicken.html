Ultimate jerk chicken instructions

  1. Combine all ingredients in a food processor or blender, blend until all the ingredients are well mixed. Place marinade in a container (I prefer a freezer bag). Add the chicken pieces and refrigerate a MINIMUM of 12 hours I personally go for 48 hours..
  2. To cook preheat oven to 350.
  3. Take out your marinated chicken place on a foil lined sheet pan, cook for about 45 to 55 min rotating the chicken at least 4 times during the cooking process..
  4. Once the chicken has reached a minimum internal temp of 175 remove from oven and allow to cool 5 to 7 min. ENJOY!!.
