Cauliflower "Mac" and Cheese instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 375. Spray a baking dish large enough to hold cauliflower with plenty of room for the sauce with non stick spray.
  2. Pre cook cauliflower in either chicken broth or salted water for 3 minutes, drain well.
  3. Heat cream in a saucepan, add borsin cheese, dijon, lemon, hot sauce and pepper stir until mixture is smooth.
  4. Combine cheddar, romano and emmantal cheese in a bowl.
  5. Add half of cheese blend to sauce, off heat, and stir in with chives and parsley.
  6. Combine sauce with cauliflower and add to prepared pan.
  7. Top with remaining cheese.
  8. Add crispy cooked bacon.
  9. Bake until hot and cheese is melted about 15 to 20 minutes.
