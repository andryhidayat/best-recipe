Ingredients of Garlic Naan bread

  1. You need 2 cups of flour.
  2. Prepare .5 tsp of baking soda.
  3. You need .5 tsp of salt.
  4. You need 1 tsp of sugar.
  5. Prepare .5 cup of yoghurt.
  6. You need of Warm water.
  7. It's of Chopped Garlic,parsley.
  8. You need of Butter for brushing.
  9. You need of Oil.

Garlic Naan bread instructions

  1. In a bowl add flour, yoghurt, salt,sugar, oil and water mix thoroughly.
  2. Knead the dough for 5 minutes,divide into parts and roll.
  3. In a pan add butter,garlic and parsley and keep aside.
  4. Add little oil in pan and place the dough in it,allow to cook on 1 side flip the other side and brush the butter mixture,do for both sides and fry.
  5. Enjoy with sauce or beef curry.
