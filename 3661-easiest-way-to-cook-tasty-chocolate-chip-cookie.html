Ingredients of Chocolate Chip Cookie

  1. You need 100 g of Butter.
  2. It's 80 g of Sugar.
  3. It's 1 of Egg.
  4. You need 1 tsp of Vanilla Essence.
  5. You need 200 g of Flour.
  6. You need 100 g of Chocolate Chip/Walnuts.

Chocolate Chip Cookie instructions

  1. Beat butter till pale. Add sugar. Mix well..
  2. Add in egg and vanilla essence. Mix well..
  3. Add in chocolate chip and walnuts. Mix well..
  4. Add in flour and slowly mix..
  5. Break the mixture into pieces and lay it on the tray..
  6. Heat it in the oven for 15 minutes at 170 degrees C..
