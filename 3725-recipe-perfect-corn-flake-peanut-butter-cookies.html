Corn Flake Peanut Butter Cookies step by step

  1. Cook over medium heat karo syrup && sugar. Bring to a boil. Boil only long enough to become runny && all sugar granules melted into liquid. Approx 5 min. (Not too long or will be to hard).
  2. Add peanut butter until melted && smooth && creamy..
  3. Add corn flakes && stir until all cereal is covered in sauce..
  4. Form balls with either your hands or a spoon in wax paper. Set wax paper on steel cookie sheet so you can put into freezer for cookies to set..
  5. Enjoy! Beware,, INCREDIBLY EASY TO MAKE && ADDICTING!.
