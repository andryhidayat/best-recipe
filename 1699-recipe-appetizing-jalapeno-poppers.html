Jalapeno Poppers step by step

  1. Cut both end of jalapenos then cut the jalapenos in half long ways then scoop out the seeds (I recommend using a spoon or knife not your hands).
  2. Soak the cut jalapenos in water for about 1 hour (depending on the heat you want).
  3. Meanwhile, cut the bacon in half and preheat the oven to 350°.
  4. Once they are done soaking, fill the inside with the cream cheese. Then wrap in the uncooked bacon. Place in a flat pan and put in the oven until cream cheese begins to melt and get brown on edges..
  5. And you're done!.
