You can cook Uncle Tim's Simple Spaghetti using 14 ingredients and 5 steps. Here is how you cook that.

Ingredients of Uncle Tim's Simple Spaghetti

  1. You need 2 lb of ground beef (or Turkey).
  2. It's 1/4-1/2 cup of minced onion.
  3. Prepare 2 tsp of Seasoned salt.
  4. It's of 1 tsp pepper.
  5. It's 1 tsp of Soul Seasoning.
  6. Prepare 1 tbsp of Italian Seasoning.
  7. Prepare 2 of cloves garlic (chopped).
  8. You need 1/2 cup of Ketchup.
  9. You need 1 (24 oz.) of Can of Hunts Italian Sausage Pasta Sauce.
  10. You need 1 of can Hunts tomato paste.
  11. You need 1 of Box Spaghetti.
  12. It's 1 of bay leaf.
  13. It's 1/2 cup of Olive oil or Vegetable oil.
  14. Prepare 1/2 cup of Bell pepper.

Uncle Tim's Simple Spaghetti instructions

  1. Brown 2 lbs Ground beef (add a little water & salt to add tenderness to meat.
  2. Add onions, bell pepper, garlic, & dry seasonings to meat omitting the Bay leaf..
  3. Chop beef with spatula until browned. Drain off excess water. Add Ketchup, Pasta sauce, & Tomato paste. As you begin to start pasta. Cook on low heat. Stir offen. (You can pretty much stir while pasta is prepared.).
  4. Prepare 1 box of spaghetti as directed on the box, but add olive oil & bay leaf..
  5. After pasta is finished, Cool then serve, or mix and store..
