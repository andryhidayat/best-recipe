Gammon Leftovers Pasta step by step

  1. Boil your pasta according to the instructions on your packet..
  2. Fry the garlic slices in olive oil gently for a few minutes then add your diced tomato and leftover gammon. Add your balsamic and continue to cook for a few minutes..
  3. Add the tomato sauce and let it all simmer on the hob for 5 minutes. Drain your pasta, combine the two and season with salt and pepper. Serve!.
