Ingredients of Carrot ginger soup

  1. Prepare 1/4 cup of butter or coconut oil.
  2. It's 1 cup of onion, chopped.
  3. It's 3 cup of carrots (peeled and chopped).
  4. Prepare 1 of large potato (peeled and cubed) or 1 cup cooked lentils.
  5. You need 1 of celery.
  6. You need 3 cup of chicken broth or vegetable broth.
  7. You need 1 tsp of ground or fresh ginger.
  8. You need 1/2 cup of milk or coconut milk.
  9. It's 1 tsp of curry powder.
  10. Prepare 1/2 tsp of salt.
  11. It's 1 of black pepper (to taste).
  12. Prepare t of tsp thyme.
  13. Prepare 2 cup of butternut squash (optional).

Carrot ginger soup instructions

  1. Heat butter over low/medium heat, saute onions until translucent.
  2. Add carrots, potato, celery, broth and ginger. Cover and bring to boil. Reduce to low heat, simmer for approx 30 min or until vegetables tender. Stir occasionally..
  3. Blend until smooth. Stir in milk, curry, salt and pepper. Simmer over low heat until heated through and reaches desires consistency (approx 10 min).
