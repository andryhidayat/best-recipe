Cream Cheese Mac And Cheese instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F. Cook macaroni in pot for 3 minutes. Drain and return to pot. Drizzle with EVOO and toss.
  2. Butter a 10x15 inch baking dish. In a large bowl whisk cream, half and half, milk, eggs, nutmeg, salt and pepper together. Stir in shredded cheese and macaroni. Spread in baking dish and scatter 1/2 inch cubes of cream cheese on top.
  3. Bake macaroni for 5 minutes. Spread melted cream cheese evenly. Bake got 40 min.
  4. Broil Mac and cheese for 2 min.
