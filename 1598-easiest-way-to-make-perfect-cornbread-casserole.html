Ingredients of Cornbread Casserole

  1. You need 1 Pound of Sweet Italian Sausage.

  3. You need 1 of Large Onion, diced.
  4. It's 2 cloves of Garlic, diced.
  5. Prepare 1 Tsp of Ground Black Pepper.

  7. Prepare 1 Tsp of Ground Basil.

Cornbread Casserole instructions

  1. Put the broiler on in your oven. Cook the Italian Sausage 7 minutes on each other. When done, let it cool and slice it up into one inch pieces..
  2. In a medium pot over medium heat, add olive oil to the pot. Put Garlic into the pot and brown up the pieces..
  3. When the garlic is browned, add the onions. Cook the Onions until they are slightly translucent..
  4. Next, add the peppers to the pot. When the peppers are tender, add sausage to the pot. Cook everything together for 5 minutes. After 5 minutes, set the Sausage & Peppers to the side to cool..
  5. Preheat the oven to 400 Degrees..
  6. Grease a 13x9 Casserole Dish, then add the Sausage & Peppers..
  7. Mix the Jiffy Corn Muffin according to the box. Pour the mixture over the Sausage & Peppers, then spread the mixture over the Sausage & Peppers..
  8. Place the Casserole Dish into the oven and cook for 20-30 minutes. Dip a toothpick into the cornbread mixture and if it comes out clean, then it is ready..
  9. Serve and Enjoy!.
