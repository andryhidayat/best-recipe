Ingredients of Lemon orzo primavera

  1. Prepare 1 tbsp of extra virgin olive oil.
  2. Prepare 1 cup of orzo pasta.
  3. Prepare 1 medium of zucchini, grated.
  4. It's 1 medium of carrot, grated.
  5. You need 1 of zest of 1 lemon.
  6. Prepare 1 can of 14.5 oz chicken or veggie broth.
  7. It's 1 1/2 tsp of lemon thyme or reg thyme.
  8. It's 1 of parmesan, optional.

Lemon orzo primavera instructions

  1. in Medium sauce pan heat oil on Med hi.
  2. add cup of orzo, cook until lightly toasted, 2 min.
  3. add zucchini and carrot, cook two mins.
  4. add broth and zest.
  5. bring to boil cook 10mins and until broth is absorbed.
  6. stir occasionally.
  7. stir in thyme and parmesan.
