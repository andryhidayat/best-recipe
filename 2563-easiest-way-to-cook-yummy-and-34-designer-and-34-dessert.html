Ingredients of "Designer" dessert

  1. Prepare 150 g of puff pastry.
  2. It's 100 g of curd made of cow milk.
  3. You need 1 tbs of sour cream.
  4. You need 2 tbs of sugar.
  5. You need 20 of graisins soaked in water.
  6. It's of peel of a lemon.
  7. It's 1 tsp of cinnamon.
  8. Prepare 100 g of poppy seeds.
  9. Prepare 1 cup of milk.

"Designer" dessert instructions

  1. Cut 4 small triangles of the puff pastry and bake them in the oven till ready (put weight on them to make sure they will be baked bful flat).
  2. Next lets cook the poppy seeds in the milk with sugar and cinnamon.
  3. Mix the cow milk curd with lemon peel and raisins and sugar.
  4. Form 2 balls of the curd and 2 from the poppy seeds. Layer it with the baked puff pastry.
