Ingredients of Unstuffed Cabbage Roll

  1. It's 11/2 of lbs. Lean Ground Beef.
  2. Prepare 1 of - Onion; chopped.
  3. Prepare 1 cup of chopped cabbage.
  4. Prepare 1/2 of - Green Pepper; chopped.
  5. You need 1 can of petite diced Tomatoes.
  6. It's 1/3 cup of Jasmine Rice; cooked and cooled.
  7. Prepare 1 Tsp. of Garlic Powder.
  8. Prepare 1 Tsp. of Turmeric.
  9. It's 1/2 Tsp. of Black Pepper.
  10. You need Pinch of Salt.

Unstuffed Cabbage Roll instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
  2. Cook rice; either by an instant pot or boiling method....let cool down..
  3. In a Dutch oven pot or a casserole dish.
  4. , mix together all ingredients well...
  5. Put dutch oven pot or a casserole dish in oven and cook for 30 minutes; remove from oven drain off liquid and stir; return to oven for 30 more minutes; remove from oven and drain again and stir; return to oven and cook another 30 minutes..
  6. Remove from oven and let it cool down a bit; then serve......*when done it will look like a casserole....
