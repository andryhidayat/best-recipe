Ingredients of Smoked Whiskey Pickle Grilled Chicken Salad

  1. It's 2 of Large boneless skinless chicken breast.
  2. Prepare 2 cups of baby spinach and Kale mix.
  3. You need 4 of tomato large wedges.
  4. It's 1/4 cup of diced cheddar cheese.
  5. Prepare 1/4 cup of rough cut white onion.
  6. It's 4 of Spears of Smoked Whiskey Pickles.
  7. It's of Red wine vinegar.
  8. Prepare of Salad vinegar.
  9. You need of Balsamic vinegar.
  10. You need of Olive oil.
  11. Prepare Pinch of spicy salt.
  12. Prepare Splash of any hot sauce of your liking.

Smoked Whiskey Pickle Grilled Chicken Salad instructions

  1. Grill chicken and rough cube cut.
  2. Rough cut all other ingredients for salad.
  3. Splash greens with hot sauce.
  4. Make salad.
  5. Make your vinaigrette or use any type of dressing you like. I use this my homemade hot salt and some generic salad season. Add your SMOKED WHISKEY PICKLES NOW..
  6. Enjoy this healthy hearty smokey salad..
