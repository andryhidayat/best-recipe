Ingredients of Rice Cooker Indonesian Yellow Rice

  1. It's 2 cup of uncooked Jasmine rice.
  2. It's 25 ml of turmeric water (from 5 cm fresh turmeric, grilled, grated, mixed with 25 ml water, strained).
  3. You need 1 tsp of turmeric powder.
  4. It's 2 cups of ml instant coconut milk.
  5. Prepare 2 cups of water.
  6. You need 1 of lemongrass, take the white part only, pounded.
  7. You need 2 of Indonesin bay leaves.
  8. Prepare 1 of pandan leaf.
  9. Prepare 1 tsp of salt or to taste.

Rice Cooker Indonesian Yellow Rice instructions

  2. Add coconut milk, pandan leaf, lemongrass, Indonesian bay leaves, and salt..
  3. Push the cooking button on the rice cooker..
