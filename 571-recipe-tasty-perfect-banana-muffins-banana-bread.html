Ingredients of Perfect BANANA Muffins : Banana Bread

  7. It's 3 tsp of ground cinnamon.

Perfect BANANA Muffins : Banana Bread step by step

  1. Chop nuts e.g. in Thermomix® blender speed 4.5 for 4 sec and set aside in bowl.
  2. Peel, add and process 3-4 bananas speed 4 for 2 sec.
  3. Add sugar and oil, mix speed 3-4 for 5 sec.
  4. Add flour, baking powder and cinnamon. Combine well at speed 4 for 10 sec.
  5. Add 50g of the chopped walnuts and stir in reverse speed 3.5 for 3 sec.
  6. I filled 24 silicon muffin forms with this amount of dough.
  7. Sprinkle top of muffins with the left-over chopped walnuts and if you'd like a tiny amount of brown sugar.
  8. Bake at 200℃ middle rack for 20 minutes. May vary +/- 1-5 min depending on your oven, but don't let them get too dark on top or to dry..
  9. BBC's original recipe recommends: add to greased and lined 2lb loaf tin. I am using a Christmas tree silicone shape for half the dough..
  10. In my tree shape I only baked it for 20 minutes all together..
  11. BBC recommends: Bake loaf tin in 200℃ oven for 20 min and covered with tin foil another 20..
  12. Allow to cool a bit before dusting with powdered sugar. Enjoy freshly baked or the day after with a more chewy taste to it..
