Ingredients of Sammie's Game Day Guacamole

  2. It's 4 tbsp of fresh chopped cilantro.
  3. Prepare 1 tsp of granulated garlic.
  4. You need 1/2 tsp of salt & pepper.
  5. You need 1 large of lime.

Sammie's Game Day Guacamole instructions

  1. Chop avacado into small pieces and put in large bowl..
  2. Mash with a fork to desired consistency..
  3. Add spices and cilantro. Mix well..
  4. Add the juice of 1 lg lime and mix well..
  5. Transfer into a serving dish and serve with chips or other dishes..
