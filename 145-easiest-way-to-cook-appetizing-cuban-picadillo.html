Ingredients of Cuban picadillo

  1. You need of ingredients.
  2. You need 2 lb of ground beef.
  3. Prepare 2 tbsp of oregano.
  4. It's 1 tbsp of cumin.
  5. You need 8 clove of garlic minced.
  6. Prepare 2 of small white onions chopped finely.
  7. It's 2 of small green peppers chopped.
  8. You need 1 cup of beefstock.
  9. You need 1 1/2 cup of tomato sauce.
  10. Prepare 4 of small potatoes peeled and cubed.
  11. It's 20 of pitted green olives.
  12. It's 1 of salt and pepper to taste.
  13. Prepare 1 of olive oil to taste.
  14. Prepare 1/2 cup of white wine.
  15. It's 1/2 cup of raisins optional.

Picadillo is a traditional dish of Latin America. Every region and area has their own way of making it. This particular recipe is geared towards Cuban Picadillo. I read a quote that compared Cuban Picadillo to be the equivalent of American Sloppy Joes.

Cuban picadillo instructions

  1. Add ground beef oregano cumin salt and pepper to a mixing bowl and mix.
  2. Saute onions peppers and garlic in oil till soft.
  3. Add ground beef mixture beefstock and tomato sauce and wine cook 15 minutes.
  4. Add potatoes cook about 15 minutes or until potatoes are done add olive and raisins if used cook till liquid is gone but meat is still moist. serve with rice or let cool use as a filler for empanadas..
