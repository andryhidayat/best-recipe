Sooji Veggie mini squares step by step

  1. Heat 1 tbsp oil in a pan. Add 1 cup chopped carrots, 1 cup shredded cabbage, and 1 cup fresh green peas. Add salt and let the veggies cook..
  2. Heat 1 tbsp oil in a pan. Add 1 tsp mustard seeds and let them crackle. Add 2-3 chopped green chilies, 1 cup chopped onions, 1 tbsp lemon juice and let them turn soft and pink..
  3. Add 1 cup semolina and roast it well. Add 2 cups water, salt as per taste, mix it well, cover and let the semolina cook..
  4. Add chopped 1 tbsp coriander leaves and mix. Then remove from flame and let it cool. Then remove the green chilies..
  5. Divide the semolina mixture in 2 balls. Place a plastic on the flat surface and roll the two balls in square shape..
  6. Then spread the veggies and grated cheese on one of the semolina square, place the other square on the veggies and flatten further using rolling pin..
  7. Cut in square shapes and place it in the refrigerator for about 15-20 minutes..
  8. Remove from the refrigerator, coat with bread crumbs and shallow fry on both the sides using oil. Serve hot..
