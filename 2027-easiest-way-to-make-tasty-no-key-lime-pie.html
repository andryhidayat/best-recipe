Ingredients of (no key) lime pie

  1. You need of Sable.
  2. You need 180 g of unsalted butter, cut in small cubed.
  3. Prepare 360 g of plain flour.
  4. It's 140 g of icing sugar.
  5. Prepare 50 g of hazelnut flour (could substitute almonds).
  6. You need 75 g of whole eggs.
  7. Prepare Pinch of salt.
  8. It's of Filling.
  9. Prepare 400 g of sweetened condensed milk.
  10. It's 4 of limes+1 for decoration.
  11. You need 4 of egg yolks (L).
  12. Prepare of Either whipping cream or meringue for decorating.
