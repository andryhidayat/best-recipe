Ingredients of Tortellini tomato soup

  1. Prepare 1 lb of ground Italian sausage (I use turkey sausage).
  2. You need 1/2 of onion (diced).
  3. You need 1 tbsp of minced garlic.
  4. It's 28 oz of crushed tomatoes.
  5. You need 32 oz of vegetable or chicken stock.
  6. It's 2 tbsp of fresh basil (chopped).
  7. Prepare 1 package of tortellini.
  8. Prepare 1 cup of packed spinach.
  9. You need of Salt and pepper.

Tortellini tomato soup instructions

  1. Heat a large pot over medium heat. Add sausage and cook until almost browned, add onion and seasoning, and cook until translucent. Add garlic and cook 1-2 minutes..
  2. Add tomatoes, broth, basil, salt and pepper. Bring to a boil, reduce heat to low and cover, simmer 30 minutes..
  3. Add tortellini and spinach and simmer about 10 minutes. Add salt and pepper to taste. Serve..
