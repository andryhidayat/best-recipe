Oven Baked Cheesy Sloppy Joe and Biscuit Casserole #mommasrecipes instructions

  1. .
  2. Mix seasonings, green peppers, red peppers in ground beef.
  3. Brown until done then drain fat off. Preheat oven 350 degrees about 25 min. Then turn off.
  4. Mix in cream cheese, sour cream, sloppy Joe sauce and extra seasoning and sugar if desire. Let simmer about 30 min..
  5. Pull bisuits apart in halves and press in bottom of pan, butter biscuits.
  6. Make sure oven is warm but turned off and place the biscuits in oven to rise for about 10-15 minutes. Let cool.
  7. Turn oven back on to 375 degrees. Let biscuits bake lightly brown. Let cool..
  8. Spread meat sauce of biscuits, cheese. Take biscuits and pull apart in halves spread on top of cheese butter. Repeat step 6..
  9. Turn oven back on to 375 degrees. Let bake for about 45 minutes or until biscuits are golden brown..
  10. Let cool.
  11. Cut and eat! Serve with any vegetable you desire. Enjoy!!!!.
