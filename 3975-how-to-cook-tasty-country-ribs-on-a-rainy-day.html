Ingredients of Country Ribs on a Rainy Day

  1. It's 4 of pork country ribs.
  2. Prepare 1 tbsp of salt.
  3. Prepare 1/2 tbsp of black pepper.
  4. Prepare 1/2 tbsp of garlic powder.
  5. It's 1/2 tbsp of onion powder.
  6. Prepare 1/2 tbsp of Turkish Paprika.
  7. You need 1 cup of Sweet Baby Rays BBQ Sauce.

Country Ribs on a Rainy Day instructions

  1. Combine all the dry ingredients in a bowl & mix & rub thoroughly on the ribs..
  2. Enclose ribs in double folded aluminum foil on baking tray & put in 350° preheated oven for 1 hour.
  3. Remove ribs from oven & put oven on broil. Brush ribs generously with BBQ sauce & put under broiler on fresh aluminum foil..
  4. Keep close eye on ribs as not to burn. Keep turning & brushing with BBQ sauce until you have a nice glaze..
  5. Remove from oven & brush with BBQ sauce one last time & serve..
