Ingredients of Lentil soup

  8. It's to taste of Salt and pepper.

Lentil soup step by step

  1. Put lentils, onion, tomatoes, potato, and salt and pepper in a pot. Cover with water and bring to boil. Simmer for 30 minutes.
  2. Fry cumin and garlic in oil till it darkens.
  3. Puree contents of pot in a blender then add garlic cumin mix. Bring to boil one more time then take off heat.
