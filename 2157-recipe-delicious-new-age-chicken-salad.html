Ingredients of New age chicken salad

  1. It's 2 of large cooked chicken breasts coarsly chopped.
  2. Prepare 1 stalk of celery finely chopped.
  3. You need 1 1/2 cup of kale leafs chopped.
  4. It's 3 tablespoon of salad dressing or mayo (I've used miracle whip).
  5. It's 1/2 teaspoon of tumeric (curcuma).
  6. It's 1/4 teaspoon of paprika.
  7. Prepare to taste of fresh ground black pepper.
