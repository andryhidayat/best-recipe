Ingredients of Leftover Ham with Cheese and Potatoes over rice

  1. It's 200 grams of Ham, diced.
  2. It's 1/2 cup of cheese spread.
  3. Prepare 2 medium of potatoes, cubed.
  4. It's 1 small of carrot, minced.
  5. It's 1 small of garlic, minced.
  6. You need 1 small of white onion, minced.
  7. It's 1/2 tsp of dried sage.
  8. Prepare 1 piece of beef bouillion cube.
  9. Prepare 2 cup of water.
  10. You need 1/2 cup of butter.
  11. Prepare 1 of salt and pepper.
  12. It's 5 cup of cooked rice.

Leftover Ham with Cheese and Potatoes over rice step by step

  1. Heat butter on a pan. Sautee garlic, onions and ham..
  2. Add potatoes, carrots, sage and salt and pepper..
  3. Add water and bring to simmer. Add beef bouillion cube. Simmer until vegetables are cooked..
  4. Taste. Add more salt, pepper or sage if desired. Serve over rice..
