Ingredients of Pizza Mac Salad

  1. Prepare 1 box of large elbow macaroni; blanch and shock.
  2. Prepare 2 1/2 cup of pizza sauce/marinara/pasta sauce.
  3. Prepare 1 cup of pepperoni; chiffonade.
  4. It's 1 cup of cooked pizza sausage; sliced in half.
  5. You need 1 cup of grated parmesan cheese.
  6. It's 1 cup of shredded mozzarella or pizza cheese blend.
  7. Prepare 2 of handfuls baby spinach; chiffonade.
  8. Prepare 1 of large pinch Italian seasoning.
  9. It's 1 of salt and pepper to taste.
