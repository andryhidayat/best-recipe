Ingredients of Ham, with Black Eyed Peas and Yellow Rice

  4. It's 1 cup of ham, ham fat with skin, and bone marrow.
  5. Prepare of Black Eyed Peas-------------.
  6. It's 1 pound of dried black eyed peas.
  7. Prepare 1 teaspoon of salt.

  9. It's 3 quarts of water +1 pint.

Ham, with Black Eyed Peas and Yellow Rice step by step

  1. Cook the rice with the cup of ham fat, marrow, and skin..
  2. Stir occasionally. When the water is absorbed and craters form cover take off the heat and don't disturb for 20 minutes. Don't peek do not lift that lid..
  3. Add the shank to 3 quarts of water.
  4. Wash and remove any bad peas and any foreign objects, like rocks and so on....
  5. When the shank has boiled for 30 minutes add the peas..
  6. Let them boil about 90 minutes stirring occasionally.
  7. Add the pint of water and bring back to boil. Let it boil 10 minutes. Cover and let sit 15 minutes..
  8. Add rice to a bowl.
  9. Add black eyed peas to the bowl. If you want to add the hot vinegar sauce now is the time..
  10. I hope you enjoy!.
