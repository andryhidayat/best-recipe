Ingredients of Watermelon radish salad (Healthy)

  1. Prepare 3 each of watermelon radishes.
  2. It's 1 of lemon.
  3. Prepare 1 bunch of of kale.
  4. Prepare 3 each of Swiss chard.
  5. It's 1/4 of purple onion dice.
  6. Prepare of olive oil.
  7. Prepare of honey.

Watermelon radish salad (Healthy) step by step

  2. Now in a bowl squeeze lemon add honey and then add the oil till it is a vinegrett consistency.
  3. Now add the dressing and toss and serve.
