Ingredients of Day After Thanksgiving Turkey Salad with Apples, Dried Cranberries & Other Stuff

  1. You need 4 cups of loosely shredded or chopped roast turkey.
  2. It's 1/4-1/3 cup of minced onion.
  3. Prepare 1/2 cup of diced fresh apple.
  4. Prepare 1/4-1/3 cup of dried cranberries (raisins, dried cherries, and chopped dried apricots would also work great).
  5. Prepare 3 Tablespoons of to 1/4 cup chopped fresh herbs (whatever you like and have on hand words) or use 1 to 2 teaspoons dried.
  6. You need 1/4-1/3 cup of chopped pickled peppers (if you only have regular cucumber pickles, that's fine, too).
  7. You need 3/4-1 cup of mayonnaise depending on creamy you like it.
  8. It's 1 Tablespoon of mustard.
  9. You need 1 Tablespoon of pickle juice (from whichever pickles you ended up using).
  10. You need 1/2 teaspoon of black pepper.
  11. It's of salt to taste (you probably won't need much between the seasoning on your turkey and the salt in the mayo, mustard, pickles).
