Labor Day Weekend Baked Beans step by step

  1. Preheat oven 350 degrees Fahrenheit.
  2. Chop the onion and pepper..
  3. Slice the bacon..
  4. Add bacon to a pan then render the fat. Add the peppers and onions..
  5. Someone went to the store and picked up two different cans of beans..
  6. Mix the sugar, ketchup, Apple cider vinegar, mustard, spices, Worcestershire sauce and liquid smoke..
  7. Add to the bacon, onions, and peppers. Simmer 3 minutes..
  8. Pour the beans in the bacon mixture..
  9. Simmer on the stove for 15 minutes..
  10. Put into oven covered for 45-50 minutes..
  11. Let rest 10 minutes serve. I hope you enjoy!!!.
