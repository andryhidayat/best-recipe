Ingredients of Arroz con habichuelas

  1. You need 2 clove of garlic.
  2. You need 1 small of onion, chopped.
  3. Prepare 2 slice of ham (or bacon).
  4. It's 2 cup of jasmine rice.
  5. Prepare 2 tbsp of knorr suiza caldo de pollo (or chicken bouillon).
  6. You need 2 tbsp of recaito (cilantro cooking base).
  7. You need pinch of dried parsley.
  8. Prepare 1 envelope of sazon goya culantro y achiote.
  9. It's 1 can of habichuela (pink beans, red beans).
  10. Prepare 1 tbsp of olive oil.
  11. You need 2 tbsp of tomato sauce.
  12. You need 2 cup of water.
