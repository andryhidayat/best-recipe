Whole Lentil Soup with Spaghetti.

You can cook Whole Lentil Soup with Spaghetti using 16 ingredients and 16 steps. Here is how you cook that.

Ingredients of Whole Lentil Soup with Spaghetti

  1. You need 1/2 cup of boiled Chick peas.
  2. Prepare 1/2 cup of Whole Red Lentil (soaked overnight).
  3. You need 100 gram of Spaghetti.
  4. Prepare 2 of big Onions.
  5. You need 1 cup of Spinach roughly chopped.
  6. You need 1/3 cup of Coriander leaves roughly chopped.
  7. You need 1/3 cup of Mint leaves roughly chopped.
  8. Prepare 3-4 of crushed Garlic pods.
  9. You need 1 of Green chilli chopped.
  10. Prepare 2 tablespoon of Olive oil.
  11. You need 2 tablespoon of Butter.
  12. You need 2 glass of Vegetable stock/Water.
  13. It's to taste of Salt.
  14. Prepare 1/2 teaspoon of Crushed Black Pepper.
  15. Prepare 2-3 tablespoon of Sour cream.
  16. You need as needed of bread sliced of your choice.

Whole Lentil Soup with Spaghetti step by step

  1. Chop 1 onion into small pieces and slice another onion into thin pieces..
  2. Heat a saucepan with olive oil and saute chopped onion for a minute..
  3. Add chopped garlic and saute for few seconds..
  4. Add turmeric powder and mix..
  5. Next add soaked red whole lentil, boiled chick peas and chopped green chilli. Give a good mix..
  6. Pour hot vegetable stock or water..
  7. Add salt and simmer for about 1/2 an hour to cook lentils completely..
  8. In the meantime heat butter in a pan and start frying thinly sliced onion..
  9. Fry till onions become golden brown and crisp..
  10. Transfer the crisp onion in a paper towel..
  11. In the same pan toast some bread slices with the leftover onion flavoured butter..
  12. Keep aside..
  13. When lentils cooks completely slide the spaghetti in the hot soup..
  14. When the spaghetti becomes soft add the chopped herbs. Cook for another 5 minutes..
  15. Before removing sprinkle 1/2 teaspoon black pepper, check the seasoning. Add salt if necessary..
  16. Transfer the soup into serving bowls, top with some sour cream and brown onion. Serve with toasted bread slices..
