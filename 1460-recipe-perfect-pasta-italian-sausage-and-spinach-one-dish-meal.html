Pasta, italian sausage and spinach one dish meal step by step

  1. add meat to pot/dutch oven, once it starts to release oil add onion to meat, cook until browned.
  2. after meat browns drain grease leaving a tbsp of grease.
  3. add chicken broth/stock and bring to a boil, once at a boil add noodles and cook until al dente about 8 mins.
  4. make your alfredo sauce while you are waiting for noodles to finish.
  5. once the noodles are done add spinach and cover, cook about 3 more mins.
  6. now add your alfredo sauce (that you made prior, made according to pack).
  7. turn fire down to simmer, mix up and let stand 5-10 mins.
  8. serve with crusty bread or french bread bon appetit!!.
