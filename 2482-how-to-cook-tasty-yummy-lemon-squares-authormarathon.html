Yummy Lemon  Squares #authormarathon step by step

  1. Turn the oven to 180 degrees. First we will make the pastry crust. Beat the margarine or butter with an electric mixer or by hand..
  2. Add 1/4 cup sugar and beat..
  3. Add the 1 cup of flour and mix till the mixture is crumbly.
  4. Press the mixture into an ungreased 8”by 8” pan. I spread this up the sides of the pan. It works better with just having the mixture on the bottom. Bake for 15 minutes till just golden..
  5. For the filling, in a small bowl combine the eggs, 3/4 cup sugar and 2 Tablespoons flour..
  6. Grate the lemon rind to get the lemon peel and add this to the mixture..
  7. Add the lemon juice and baking powder. Beat the mixture..
  8. Pour filling over baked layer. Bake for 20 minutes. Done when edges are slightly brown and the center is set..
  9. Cool on a wire rack. Cut into squares and sprinkle icing sugar if desired. Enjoy!.
