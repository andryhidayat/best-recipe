easy lil poppers step by step

  1. spray cookie sheets or pans you will use with non stick spray, take a bisquit and flatten it, place in the middle about 1/2 to 1 tsp of softened cream cheese, a few pieces of chiles and a couple pieces of bacon..
  2. fold the edges of the bisquits over to make a bread ball/ pouch.
  3. place them on bake sheet about 2 inches apart. bake in oven at 350' for about 10-15 minutes depending on your oven..
  4. let sit for a few minutes when done to let cool..
