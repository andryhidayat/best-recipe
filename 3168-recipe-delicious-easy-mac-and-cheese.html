Ingredients of Easy Mac and Cheese

  1. Prepare 1/4 of Velveeta Cheese.
  2. It's 1 tsp of salt and pepper.
  3. Prepare 1/2 of milk.
  4. Prepare 2 cups of cut macaroni.
  5. You need 2 cups of shredded Cheddar Cheese.

Easy Mac and Cheese instructions

  1. Cook macaroni per box and drain..
  2. Return macaroni to pot..
  3. Mix shredded cheese, milk, salt and pepper and cheddar cheese into the pot with the macaroni..
  4. Mix until blended well and Velveeta has completely melted. Set the pot on simmer!.
  5. Enjoy!.
