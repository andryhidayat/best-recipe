Delicious Mac n Cheese step by step

  1. brown hamburger meat (pepper to taste).
  2. add prego to cooked hamburger meat.
  3. in separate pot boil water and cook Mac n cheese as normal.
  4. Combine Mac n cheese with hamburger meat and sauce.
  5. Place mixture in casserole dish and cover with mozzarella cheese.
  6. Bake at 375 for 10-15 minutes or until cheese is melted.
