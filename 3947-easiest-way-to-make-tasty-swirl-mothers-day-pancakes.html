Swirl mothers day pancakes step by step

  1. Pre-mix instant pancake mix to the consistency you like. I use a fork to beat it..
  2. Add food coloring, one drop of each color is plenty..
  3. Lightly move batter around to get that swirl..
  4. Heat skillet on medium heat. Add a little vegetable oil, just enough to coat the pan..
  5. Pour enough batter in the skillet to make decent size pancakes..
  6. Flip when its bubbling. Cook til golden brown..
