Ground Beef.

You can have Ground Beef using 5 ingredients and 10 steps. Here is how you cook it.

Ingredients of Ground Beef

  1. It's 1 lb of Ground beef.
  2. Prepare 1 of Garlic salt.
  3. It's 1 of Lemon pepper.
  4. You need 1 of Salt.
  5. Prepare 1 of Pepper.

Ground Beef instructions

  1. Spray large pan with cooking spray.
  2. Put ground beef in pan.
  3. Break into pieces with a big spoon.
  4. Add a bit of all seasonings* to meat. (*Equal amounts of each seasoning).
  5. Stir and mix the meat to mix all the seasonings..
  6. Let it start to brown for 3-5 minutes.
  7. Put more garlic salt and lemon pepper. Then stir and let sit again.
  8. When you see the meat is all brown, with no red, taste to see if you need more seasoning. Add any or all as needed. Then mix and let sit again.
  9. Turn off the stove. Take the pan to the sink. Cover the pan with a lid and drain the grease. Hold on tight to the pan! Don't let the meat fall out..
  10. Leftovers last 7 days after you cook it.
