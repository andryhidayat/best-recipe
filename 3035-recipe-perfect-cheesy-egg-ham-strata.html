Cheesy Egg Ham Strata step by step

  1. Preheat Oven 400°F.
  2. Dice 4 1/8 inch slices of ham.
  3. Mix Eggs until blended well, add milk, stir well..
  4. Toast 4 slices of bread, then butter well..
  5. Place toast in bottom of pan, press down just alittle..
  6. Sprinkle Pepper over buttered toast..
  7. Pour stirred egg mixture over toast.
  8. Using a fork press down toast, if it floats up..
  9. Sprinkle with ham over top and then, sprinkle cheese over the top..
  10. *Turn Oven down to 350°F. Put glass pan in oven..
  11. Bake for 45 minutes, check with knife, it should come out clean..
  12. It will have a nice crusty top. Let Set 5 minutes to cool. Enjoy..
