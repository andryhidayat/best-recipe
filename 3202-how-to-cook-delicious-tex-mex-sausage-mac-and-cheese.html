Ingredients of Tex Mex Sausage Mac and Cheese

  4. Prepare 1 of large bell pepper chopped.
  5. You need 1 lb of box of macaroni pasta.

  11. Prepare 1 packages of Andouille sausage.

Tex Mex Sausage Mac and Cheese step by step

  1. Boil salt water.
  2. Dice all ingredients : andouille sausage onions green peppers. Open corn and tomatoes.
  3. Cook the ground meat and after meat is ground add all the chopped ingredients until veggies are softly cooked and sausage is also cooked. (Set aside?.
  4. Cook pasta! I cooked my pasta a little al dente because it does go in the oven for a few minutes.
  5. Strain pasta set aside for it so cool mix all ingredients eggs milk meat mixture cheese mix it all together except the sprinkle cheese.
  6. Season to taste a sprinkle cheese to the top and place in the oven for about 20 minutes.
  7. Add a salad and bread and enjoy.
