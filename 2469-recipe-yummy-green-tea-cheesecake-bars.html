Ingredients of Green Tea Cheesecake Bars

  1. You need 100 g of Graham cracker or digestive biscuit.
  2. You need 60 g of Butter.
  3. You need 200 g of Cream Cheese at room temperature.
  4. Prepare 100 ml of plain Yogurt.
  5. Prepare 100 ml of Heavy Cream.
  6. Prepare 90 g of Sugar.
  7. You need 2 of Eggs at room temperature.
  8. It's 2 tbsp of Corn starch.
  9. Prepare 2 tbsp + 1 tsp of Matcha Green Tea powder.
  10. Prepare of Lemon juice, optional.

Green Tea Cheesecake Bars step by step

  1. Put cheese cloth or coffee filter in a colander and put 100ml of yogurt into it. Let the yogurt drain for about 6 hours..
  2. Preheat oven to 170C (340F). Place parchment paper in a 7 inch Ã— 8 inch square pan. Crush crackers in a plastic bag. Put crushed crackers and melted butter in a bowl, and mix it. Put it into a square pan then press with your hand..
  3. Put softened cream cheese in a blender or food processor and turn on! Add drained yogurt, sugar and heavy cream and mix. Add eggs one at a time..
  4. Add corn starch and green tea and blend!.
  5. Strain the mixture through a strainer and pour it into the pan. Bake in a water bath for 35 min to 40 minutes. Cool to room temperature and refrigerate at least 3 hours..
  6. Cut cheese cake into 14 to 16 pieces..
  7. Wrapping tips: wrap a cheese cake bar with wax paper. twist both ends of the paper..
  8. Enjoy!.
