Lemon Pepper Chicken instructions

  1. Mix Flour and Lemon pepper seasoning in a shallow bowl.
  2. Moisten chicken lightly with water.
  3. Coat evenly with the seasoned flour.
  4. Melt butter in a large non stick skillet on medium heat.
  5. Cook each side of chicken until golden brown and no longer pink inside..
  6. Plate and enjoy!.
