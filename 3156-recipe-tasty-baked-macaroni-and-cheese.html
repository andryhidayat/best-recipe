Ingredients of Baked Macaroni and Cheese

  1. You need 1 oz of Velveeta Cheese.
  2. Prepare 1 cup of milk.
  3. You need 1 cup of half and half.
  4. You need 3 cups of shredded cheese.
  5. You need 1 (13 inch) of pan.
  6. Prepare 16 oz of box of elbow macaroni.
  7. You need Dash of salt and pepper.
  8. It's 2 of eggs.

Baked Macaroni and Cheese step by step

  1. Cook macaroni per box instructions. Meanwhile preheat oven to 425 degrees..
  2. Drain macaroni put in bowl..
  3. Butter entire pan to include sides.
  4. Mix eggs, salt/pepper, Velveeta (cut into pieces) shredded cheese, milk, and half and half into bowl with macaroni. Blend.
  5. Pour mixture into pan. Add additional shredded cheese on top..
  6. Bake for 30 minutes..
