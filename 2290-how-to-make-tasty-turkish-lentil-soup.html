Ingredients of Turkish lentil soup

  1. It's 1 medium of onion.
  2. It's 1 large of potato.
  3. It's 1 of carrot.
  4. It's 2 tbsp of olive oil.
  5. It's 1 tbsp of salcha.
  6. Prepare 150 grams of red lentils.
  7. It's 1 of beef stock cube.
  8. Prepare 1 of seasoning black pepper and salt to your taste.
  9. It's 2 tbsp of butter.
  10. It's 1 pinch of pepper flakes.
  11. It's 1 of enough hot water to cover.

Turkish lentil soup step by step

  1. in a pot place add the oil then chopped onion. at a medium heat sautee them and add salcha chopped carrots and chopped potatoes and cook for another 2 minutes. add the washed and drained red lentils, seasoning and hot water and stock cube. stir well. cook on a low heat for 30-45 minutes..
  2. at the end add pepper and chilli flakes and butter and mix in..
  3. take pot off the heat and blend with a soup blender until smooth..
