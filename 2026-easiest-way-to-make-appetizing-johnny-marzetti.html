Ingredients of Johnny Marzetti

  1. You need 1 lb of ground beef, Italian sausage or mix.
  2. You need 1 of onion.
  3. It's 8 oz of mushrooms.
  4. It's 1 of green bell pepper (optional).
  5. It's 8 oz of macaroni or rotini pasta.
  6. Prepare 8 oz of shredded cheese (I use cheddar & mozzarella. Use more if you like).
  7. It's of -- Tomato Sauce --.
  8. Prepare 1 can of tomatoes (14 oz).
  9. You need 2 tsp of Italian herbs (oregano, basil, thyme, etc).
  10. Prepare 2 clove of garlic.
  11. Prepare 1 Tbsp of olive oil.

Johnny Marzetti instructions

  1. For a simple, homemade tomato sauce (skip to step 3 if using jarred sauce): Sauté garlic in some olive oil until fragrant. Add a can of tomatoes and sprinkle in a teaspoon or two of Italian herbs..
  2. Simmer for 5-10 minutes. Add salt & pepper to taste. If it seems a little acidic, add a teaspoon or two of sugar..
  3. Prepare the rest of the ingredients: Chop the onion and bell pepper, and slice the mushrooms..
  4. Preheat oven to 180C/350F. Boil pasta and drain..
  5. Sauté onion in oil until softened, about 3 minutes..
  6. Add mushrooms and fry until juices are released, about 5 minutes. If using the green peppers, sauté them along with the mushrooms too..
  7. Add beef and cook, stirring, breaking up clumps, until no longer red..
  8. Remove from heat and mix in tomato sauce and all but about 1/2 cup of cheese (you need some to top the casserole)..
  9. Transfer to a large, greased baking dish and add pasta. Toss gently to mix. Scatter remaining cheese on top (probably need more than I have here in the photo!).
  10. Bake, uncovered, until browned and bubbling (30 - 35 minutes)..
  11. Done! Dish out and enjoy!!.
