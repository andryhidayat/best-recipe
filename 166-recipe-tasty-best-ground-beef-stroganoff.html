Ingredients of Best ground beef stroganoff!

  1. It's 2 lb of ground beef, turkey or venison.
  2. You need 2 of onion, chopped.
  3. Prepare 1 1/2 tbsp of Minced Garlic.
  4. You need 8 oz of Mushrooms sliced.
  5. Prepare 2 cup of Beef broth.
  6. Prepare 1/3 cup of Butter.
  7. It's 1/3 cup of All-purpose flour.
  8. Prepare 1 tsp of Salt.
  9. Prepare 1 tsp of Ground black pepper.
  10. Prepare 2 cup of whatever noodles you want to use. I used elbow cause it was what I had.
  11. You need 1 cup of Sour cream.
  12. Prepare 4 cup of boiling water.

Best ground beef stroganoff! step by step

  1. Brown ground beef/turkey/venison, whichever you choose. Sauté onions, garlic and mushrooms. Do this all together in one large pan..
  2. Make your roux by melting 1/3 cup of butter in medium sauce pan. Once melted slowly whisk in 1/3 cup of flour adding a little at a time. Slowly add the 2 cups of beef broth continuing to whisk..
  3. Pour your roux in with your ground beef/turkey and onions, add salt and pepper and stir..
  4. Add your sour cream and stir. Put on low heat for 10 minutes..
  5. Bring your 4 cups of water to a boil. Once boiling add your 2 cups of noodles for approximately 10 minutes. I eat a few noodles (or make my wife eat a few) as I'm cooking to test their doneness. Once the noodles are how you like them strain them..
  6. This is totally optional. Add your noodles to your ground beef, onions, roux, sour cream mixture and mix it all up real good. Or just put the noodles on a plate and pour some stroganoff over it..
  7. Last but certainly not least. Enjoy the delicious Beef Stroganoff you've just poured your heart and soul into because YOU deserve it..
