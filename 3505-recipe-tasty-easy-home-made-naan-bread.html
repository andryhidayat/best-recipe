Easy home made Naan bread step by step

  1. Add 125ml warm water, 1 Tablespoon of sugar and the yeast to a bowl. Set aside for around 15 minutes until frothy..
  2. In a mixing bowl add the flour and remaining ingredients and mix well..
  3. Add the yeast mixture to the bowl once ready and start kneading until you have a nice soft dough..
  4. Cover and set aside in a warm area for around 1 hour or untill doubled in size..
  5. Divide the dough into equal sized and roll out..
  6. Cook on a hot pan for around 4-5 minutes a side..
  7. Once removed baste with the garlic butter and serve fresh. ðŸ‘Œ.
