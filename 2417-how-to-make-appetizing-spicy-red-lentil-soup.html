Spicy red lentil soup step by step

  1. Chop the onion, potato and carrot..
  2. Fry the onion in oil until it starts to brown..
  3. Add the potato and carrot and fry for a few more minutes stirring occasionally so it doesn’t stick..
  4. Add the spices, salt, garlic and chilli..
  5. Rinse the lentils and add to the pan along with the stock. Cook over a medium heat for about an hour, until the lentils break apart and the potatoes are soft..
  6. Serve and enjoy!.
