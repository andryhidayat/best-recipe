Adam's Italian Sausage Sandwiches step by step

  1. Slice up onion and pepper..
  2. In large skillet.. cook sausages slowly on low-med heat for about 15minutes.. turning after 10min..
  3. Increase heat to med-high heat and cook for an additional 10-15 min. stir peppers and onions.. turn sausages occasionally..
  4. Once completely cooked.. reduce heat to low and place 1 slice of provolone on each sausage. allow cheese to melt..
  5. place sausages in buns and top with onions and peppers. Drizzle on desired tomato sauce. Enjoy!! :).
