Ingredients of The Thanksgiving Day "Cupcake"

  1. It's 4 cup of leftover stuffing.
  2. You need 1 1/2 cup of leftover green bean casserole.
  3. You need 2 cup of leftover mashed potatoes.
  4. It's 1 of crushed french fried onions to crumble on top.
  5. It's 1 of heated turkey gravy to drizzle(optional).

They were delicious.especially the buttercream frosting!! The cupcake toppers I made are what you will find in a typical Thanksgiving meal - turkey and pumpkin. A perfect ending to a thanksgiving meal is the pumpkin pie. Why not make pumpkin pie cupcake to change things up a little bit?

The Thanksgiving Day "Cupcake" instructions

  1. In a greased muffin pan place stuffing only to the top. Don't mound..
  2. With a spoon that has been lightly sprayed with cooking spray, make a well or indentation in stuffing then fill with about 1 Tbsp of green bean casserole. The stuffing will rise a little. That's OK..
  3. Pipe or spoon leftover mashed potatoes on top of "cupcake" and bake in the oven at 350 for 15-20 min or until potatoes lightly brown..
  4. Add crushed French fried onions and drizzle with gravy if you want..
