Three Cheese Mac N Cheese step by step

  1. preheat oven to 350.
  2. boil water.
  3. add mac noodles and cook til they are still chewy.
  4. cut velveeta cheese.
  5. drain noodles place back in pan.
  6. add velveeta cheese and mix til its creamy.
  7. add milk.
  8. sprinkle pepper into macaroni and stir.
  9. add parmesan cheese and mix well.
  10. place macaroni into glass baking dish after you spray it with pam.
  11. smooth out macaroni and add shredded cheese to the top.
  12. place in the oven and bake for 30-40 minutes.
  13. remove from oven and enjoy the cheesey goodness.
