Lemon bars step by step

  1. Here are the ingredients.
  2. Preheat your oven and grease the baking pan.
  3. In a bowl,crumble flour, powdered sugar and butter.
  4. Pour into baking pan and gently press the crust.
  5. Bake for 20 minutes or until brown.
  6. For the egg mixture,whisk the eggs,add lemon juice and lemon zest,add a little amount of flour and sugar to thicken the mixture.
  7. When the crust is done and still hot,add the egg mixture on top and bake for another 25 minutes.
  8. Sprinkle with some powdered sugar and cut into pieces.
  9. Enjoy every bite !.
