Lentil & potato pressure cooker vegetable soup instructions

  1. Set electric pressure cooker (Instant pot or other) to brown.
  2. Melt butter and brown onions in it until they're good and caramelized on the outside..
  3. While onions are browning chop up any veggies that aren't prepared yet and the habanero pepper..
  4. Add all other ingredients to onions, including broth. Stir.
  5. Set pressure cooker to high pressure for 18 minutes.
  6. When time is up, fast release pressure and serve..
  7. Bon appetit.
