Ingredients of Chili for a chilly day

  1. It's 31 oz of can of pinto beans.
  2. Prepare 15 1/2 oz of can of Crowder peas.
  3. Prepare 15 1/2 oz of can light kidney beans.
  4. You need 15 1/2 oz of can of dark red kidney beans.
  5. It's 15 1/2 oz of can of great northern beans.
  6. It's 4 3/4 lb of 90/10 ground beef very lean.
  7. It's 1/4 cup of ground turmeric.
  8. You need 1/4 cup of ground cumin.
  9. Prepare 1/4 cup of onion powder.
  10. It's 1/4 cup of dehydrated onion flakes.
  11. It's 1/4 cup of Hungarian paprika.
  12. You need 1/4 cup of granulated garlic powder.
  13. It's 1 tbsp of smoked paprika.
  14. It's 28 oz of can of crushed tomatoes.
  15. You need 28 oz of can of tomato sauce.
  16. You need 1 1/2 tbsp of salt more if you need.
  17. Prepare 2 tbsp of red pepper flakes optional.

Chili for a chilly day instructions

  1. Brown your beef add onion flakes stir constantly. This will cause the meat to fill the whole chili not in clumps..
  2. When brown, still on heat, add beans and peas. Stir well. Add crushed tomatoes and sauce. Add all your ingredients together stir well..
  3. Let simmer stirring occasionally. Cover let sit for 15 minutes..
  4. Serve, garnish with red pepper flakes. Hope you enjoy! I also had mine with a cornbread muffin..
