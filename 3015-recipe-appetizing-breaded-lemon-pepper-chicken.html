Ingredients of Breaded Lemon Pepper Chicken

  1. Prepare 2 lb of boneless chicken breast, sliced into thinner halves.
  2. It's 1/3 cup of all purpose flour.
  3. It's 1/3 cup of panko or regular bread crumbs.
  4. Prepare 3 tbsp of lemon pepper seasoning.
  5. You need 3 tbsp of butter.

Breaded Lemon Pepper Chicken instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F..
  2. Trim fat from chicken breasts and cut them into thinner halves..
  3. Mix flour and bread crumbs into a shallow bowl, and melt butter into another..
  4. Dip chicken breasts into butter, then flour/bread crumb mixture..
  5. Sprinkle lemon pepper seasonings onto chicken breasts, adding more if desired..
  6. Spread any remaining butter into bottom of a baking dish. Place chicken breasts into pan..
  7. Bake chicken for 45 minutes. I broil for an additional 5 at the end to ensure slightly more crisp..
  8. Enjoy!.
