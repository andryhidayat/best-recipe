Lentil_soup instructions

  1. Clean lentils and wash well.
  2. Wash rice and soak for 1 hour.
  3. Dice onions finely.
  4. In a suitable pot, on high heat, add lentils and water.
  5. When boiling point has reached, reduce heat and cover the pot untill lentils are cooked.
  6. Remove lentils from heat and grind manually or using a electrical grinder.
  7. In another pot add the grinded lentils,and place on the stove.
  8. Add the rice to the pot and cover until rice has cooked.
  9. Add ghee and onions and fry in a frying pan, until golden in colour.
  10. Fried onions are added to the soup. You may add cumin and salt as desired.
  11. Leave the pot for a further 10 minutes to achieve a creamy texture.
  12. Hot water is added if needed.
  13. Lentils soup is served hot, with toasted bread and lemon juice.
