Ingredients of Lazy day lasagna

  1. It's 1 bag of all cheese frozen ravioli.
  2. It's 1 bag of meat frozen ravioli.
  3. Prepare 2 jars of spaghetti sauce.
  4. It's 1 bag of mozzarella cheese or Italian blend cheese (I use both).
  5. Prepare 2 cans of mushrooms (can leave out).
  6. Prepare to taste of Salt, pepper, garlic powder, Italian seasoning.

Lazy day lasagna instructions

  1. In crockpot put spaghetti sauce to just cover the bottom so it don’t stick..
  2. Then go around placing the ravioli. then add some cheese and mushrooms, then add more spaghetti sauce do this till all of the ravioli is used ending with a light layer of sauce and having the cheese covering the top.
  3. Cook on low 6-8 hrs or on high 2-4 hrs cooking times give or take every crockpot and slow cooker cook different..
  4. Notes- you can add any kind of veggies to this.. or cooked hamburger instead of the meat ravioli or use both. When placing ravioli can do every other one meat then cheese, or can do a layer of all cheese then next layer the meat ravioli. Can use your own spaghetti sauce or jars.
