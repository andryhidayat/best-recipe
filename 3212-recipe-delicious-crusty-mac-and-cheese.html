Crusty Mac and cheese instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 Fahrenheit.
  2. Bring water to boil in a pot and add pasta.
  3. In small pot add milk, Velveeta cheese, 3/4 cup mozzarella cheese and spices. Cook on low heat until melted..
  4. While pasta and cheese is cooking finely mince 3 cloves of garlic and half a bundle of green onions. (I usually use 1 large leek but the store was out. Leeks add a sweeter flavor compared to the bitterness of green onions).
  5. Add green onions and fresh garlic to the cheese..
  6. Once pasta is soft and cheese is melted add them to a casserole dish and mix..
  7. Add the rest of the mozzarella cheese as a layer on top of mac and cheese..
  8. Sprinkle some smoked paprika on top of that..
  9. Spread the crushed croutons on top of everything as a crust. And bake for 30 minutes at 350.
  10. Enjoy.
