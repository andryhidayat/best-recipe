EZ Fancy Mac N Cheese & Turkey Burger step by step

  1. Preheat your oven to 350Â°F..
  2. Cook up your mac according to the box directions - usually about 7 minutes..
  3. Meanwhile, cook up your ground turkey burger in a frying pan. You can use any type of meat that you desire - steak, hamburger, and chicken sausage and bacon all work well. I prefer turkey burger because it's lean and crumbles, which helps distribute it evenly throughout the dish. Add some delicious fresh or dried herbs, salt and pepper to taste..
  4. When your mac is done, drain it, add it back into your hot pot and melt your butter into the pasta. Once the butter is melted, add your packet cheese and milk (and a lil heavy cream if you have some around for added creamy flava). Add a handful or so of extra shredded cheese to make it extra cheesy. Mix it up!.
  5. Now add your cooked turkey burger (or whatever meat you made) into your cheesy mac mix. Mix it up!.
  6. Add your mac mixture into a baking dish (glass works best). Add another handful of shredded cheese on top. Now add some breadcrumbs onto the top. It's up to you how much you want to add - I like to give it a nice top so it will have a crunchy baked taste. You can also add a lil parmesan cheese on top if you want. Drizzle some olive oil over the top of your breadcrumbs and cheese so it bakes up nice..
