Chicken & Veggie Mac-N-Cheese step by step

  1. put chicken in large pot and cover with water.
  2. season water and boil chicken until fully cooked..
  3. take mixed veggies and put in small pot of water with salt and pepper. cook on Med heat until hot, then take off heat and drain. set aside..
  4. when chicken is cooked, remove from water and pour noodles from pack in water. cook until tender, then drain..
  5. remove meat from chicken..
  6. In large bowl or pot mix noodles, veggies, chicken and cheese sauce.
  7. NOW EAT & ENJOY!!!.. HOPE U LIKE IT!!.
