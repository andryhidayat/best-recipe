Ingredients of Lemon and herb chicken (nando's style)

  1. It's 1 kg of Chicken in 4 pieces(make cuts).
  2. It's of Lemon juice of 1 medium size lemon.
  3. You need 1/4 cup of Olive oil or melted butter.
  4. You need 1 tsp of Mixed herbs.
  5. It's 1 1/2 tsp of Parsley (I used dried).
  6. It's 2 tbsp of Nandos lemon and herb sauce (optional).
  7. Prepare 1 tsp of Sage (I used dried).
  8. Prepare 2 tbsp of Hung curd.
  9. Prepare 2 tsp of Freshly minced garlic.
  10. Prepare 1 1/2 tsp of Freshly crushed black pepper.
  11. Prepare to taste of Salt.
