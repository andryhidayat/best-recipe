Ingredients of Steve’s Crockpot Ground Turkey Chili

  1. You need 1 package of ground turkey.
  2. It's 1 can of corn (15 oz).
  3. Prepare 1 can of red kidney beans (15oz).
  4. Prepare 1 can of black beans (15 oz).
  5. It's 1 can of tomato sauce (28 oz).
  6. You need of Liquid smoke (8-10 squirts).
  7. You need 5 tablespoons of chili powder.
  8. Prepare 2 tablespoons of ground cumin.
  9. Prepare 2 tablespoons of ground thyme.
  10. Prepare of Season salt (to taste).

Steve’s Crockpot Ground Turkey Chili instructions

  1. Brown ground turkey. Season with season salt to taste. Drain after browning..
  2. Turn crockpot on high. Add meat to crockpot and all ingredients to crockpot..
  3. Stir and cool on high for about an hour or until it reaches a boil. Then reduce to low and allow to simmer for another 45 minutes..
  4. Let sit for about 10 minutes before serving..
