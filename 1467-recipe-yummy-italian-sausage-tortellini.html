Ingredients of Italian Sausage Tortellini

  1. You need 1 tablespoon of olive oil.
  2. It's 2 cups of diced onions.
  3. You need 1 lb. of mild Italian sausage.
  4. It's 2-3 cloves of minced garlic.
  5. You need 3/4 cup of chicken broth.
  6. It's 1 (14.5 oz) of can petite diced tomatoes.
  7. Prepare 1/2 cup of regular or heavy cream.
  8. Prepare 9 oz of tortellini (fresh or frozen).
  9. You need 1 teaspoon of salt.
  10. Prepare 1/2 teaspoon of pepper.
  11. Prepare 2 cups of fresh baby spinach.

Italian Sausage Tortellini instructions

  1. Heat olive oil in skillet over medium heat. Add onions & sausage to brown. After browning, reduce heat to low & add garlic..
  2. Add broth, tomatoes, cream & tortellini. Stir to combine. Increase heat to boil..
  3. Cover then reduce heat to simmer for 15 minutes or until tortellini is tender..
  4. Taste sauce & add salt & pepper to taste. Add spinach & stir until softened & wilted. Serve & enjoy!!.
