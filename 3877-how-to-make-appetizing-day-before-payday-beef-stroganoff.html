Ingredients of Day before payday beef stroganoff

  1. It's 1 lb of hamburger meat.
  2. Prepare 3 (10.5 oz) of cans of cream of mushroom soup.
  3. It's 2/3 cup of sour cream.
  4. It's 3/4 lb of egg noodles (3/4 bag).
  5. You need bag of frozen broccli (optional).
  6. It's to taste of salt, pepper, Italian season's & garlic power.

Day before payday beef stroganoff instructions

  1. Brown meat and drain.
  2. Mix soup, meat & broccli together & let simmer for 10 minutes. Now is when you season to taste.
  3. Boil noodles until cooked..
  4. Combine noodles, meat mixture & sour cream in a pot. Mix well..
  5. If youre feeling fancy, add parmesan cheese. Enjoy!.
