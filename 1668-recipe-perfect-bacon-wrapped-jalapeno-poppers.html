Bacon Wrapped Jalapeño Poppers step by step

  1. Preheat oven to 450°F.
  2. Slice jalapeños in half and remove seeds..
  3. Mix cheddar and cream cheese in a medium mixing bowl.
  4. Slice bacon in half.
  5. Fill jalapeños with cheese mixture.
  6. Wrap jalapeño with a piece of bacon and secure with a toothpick.
  7. Place on a foil covered bacon sheet and bake in the oven for 20 - 25 minutes or until bacon is crispy.
  8. Move preserves to microwaveable safe bowl and heat for 15 - 30 seconds, stirring occasionally until liquified.
  9. Remove the poppers from the oven and brush preserve on top.
  10. Return to oven and bake for 5 - 10 minutes, until preserve caramelizes.
  11. Remove from oven, let cool, and serve.
