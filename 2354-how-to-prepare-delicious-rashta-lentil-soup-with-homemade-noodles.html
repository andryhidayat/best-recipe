  1. Fill pot with halfway. Wash the lentil and boil it for ~45 minutes (I used the same glass to measure the lentil and the flour).
  2. Fry the onion until soft and add it to the lentil with the cumin.
  3. Mix the dough ingredients..
  4. Roll the dough and cut it into strips (like tagliatelle).
  5. Fry the garlic with the coriander in olive oil.
  6. After ~40 minutes of boiling the lentil (make sure that the lentil in cooked) add the the dough strips and the fried garlic and coriander. Turn the hob to a low - medium temperature..
