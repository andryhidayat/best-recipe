Ingredients of Lemon Honey Chicken and Carrots

  1. You need of chicken and carrots.
  2. It's 6 large of boneless and skinless chicken thighs.
  3. You need 1 large of onion sliced.
  4. Prepare 1 1/2 lb of carrots.
  5. Prepare 2 tbsp of Hungarian, paprika.
  6. You need 1 1/2 tsp of salt.
  7. It's 1 1/2 tsp of granulated garlic powder.
  8. Prepare 1/4 tsp of ground black pepper.
  9. You need of honey lemon mixture.
  10. It's 1/4 cup of honey.
  11. Prepare 1/4 cup of lemon juice.
  12. You need 1/4 cup of water.
  13. You need 1/4 cup of olive oil, extra virgin.
  14. It's of topping.
  15. It's 1/4 tsp of whole leaf oregano.
  16. It's 1/2 tbsp of cilantro.
  17. Prepare 1/2 tbsp of chopped parsley.

Lemon Honey Chicken and Carrots step by step

  1. Preheat oven 400° Fahrenheit.
  2. Set all the chicken and carrots ingredients into a deep pan.
  3. Mix the honey, lemon juice, water and olive oil. Pour on top. Add oregano, cilantro, and parsley..
  4. Bake in oven 45 minutes covered, then uncover and roast in oven for 30 minutes more..
  5. Let sit 5 minutes. Serve hope you enjoy!.
