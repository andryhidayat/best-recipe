Oreo cookies n cream poke cake step by step

  1. Make French vanilla cake as directed on package.
  2. Once cake comes out of the oven, let it cool.
  3. Prepare the oreo pudding as directed on package.
  4. Poke holes thru out cake.
  5. Pour pudding over the cake while the pudding is still not thick.
  6. Put cake in fridge to chill and set for 2 hours.
  7. After cake has chilled, take some oreos, crunch them up.
  8. mix crunched up oreos with whipped topping.
  9. Spread the oreo and whipped topping mixture over cake.
