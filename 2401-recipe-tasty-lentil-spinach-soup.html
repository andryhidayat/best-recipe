Ingredients of Lentil spinach soup

  1. It's 1/2 cup of washed split red lentil.
  2. Prepare 1/2 cup of chopped spinach.
  3. It's 1/2 of chopped tomato.
  4. You need 1/4 teaspoon of turmeric powder.
  5. Prepare 2 tablespoon of roasted and crushed peanut.
  6. Prepare to taste of Salt.
  7. Prepare 1 teaspoon of green chilli paste.
  8. Prepare 4 of curry leaves.
  9. You need 1 tablespoon of oil.

Lentil spinach soup step by step

  1. Take a pressure cooker add red lentil, chopped spinach, chopped tomato, salt turmeric powder and 1 1/2 cup water mix them well..
  2. Pressure cook for 2 to 3 whistles..
  3. Heat oil in a another pan add green chilli paste and curry leaves and cook for 1 minute..
  4. Add boiled lentil mixture salt, crushed peanut and water mix it well bring to boil..
  5. Cook on a low flame for few minutes..
  6. Lentil spinach soup is ready..
