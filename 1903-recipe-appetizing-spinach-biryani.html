Spinach biryani instructions

  1. In a grinder, grind spinach, green chilles, ginger garlic paste with little water into a smooth paste..
  2. Soak jasmince rice in water for about 30 mins.
  3. Heat a deep bottom pan with ghee. Add bay leaf, cardamom, cloves, elachi, cumin, cinnamon for 2 mins till aroma comes..
  4. Add the spinach puree and cook for 5 mins..
  5. Add the soaked rice and add 2 cups of water and salt according to taste. You can add veggies too and add half cup of water for veggies..
  6. Cook on medium flame for about 15 mins until water evaporates and rice is cooked..
  7. In a separate pan, add oil and fry the sliced onions till caramelised brown..
  8. Top the rice with caramelized onions. And serve hot and enjoy with raita.
