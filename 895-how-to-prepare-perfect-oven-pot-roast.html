Ingredients of Oven Pot Roast

  1. Prepare 3 of or 4 pound roast.
  2. Prepare 6 of to 8 potatoes ( any kind is fine).
  3. It's 2 of bags baby carrots.
  4. You need 2 cup of water.
  5. You need 1 large of onion.
  6. Prepare 1 of Meat tenderizer.
  7. You need 1 of Garlic powder.
  8. You need 1 of Parsley flakes.
  9. You need 1 of Salt & pepper.
  10. You need 1 of Roaster pan.

Oven Pot Roast step by step

  1. Preheat oven to 350.
  2. Peel potatoes, cuts into big chunks the rinse..
  3. Rinse roast and then place in the roaster pan, rub with meat tenderizer, and sprinkle garlic powder over roast..
  4. Place potatoes on one side of the roast, rinse carrots and then place on the other side of roaster pan..
  5. Sprinkle parsley flakes over potatoes, roast and carrots..
  6. Rinse onion then slice over meat and veggies..
  7. Place in oven with a secure lid..
  8. Bake for 2 - 2 1/2 hours. Depends on the weight of the roast..
