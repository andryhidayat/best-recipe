Ingredients of No sugar added gluten free Blueberry carrot protein cake

  1. Prepare 1 cup of white jasmine rice flour.
  2. You need 45 grams of garden of life raw fit unflavored high protein powder.
  3. It's 1 1/2 cup of finely pureed carrots.
  4. Prepare 1 cup of blueberries.
  5. Prepare 3 of eggs.

  9. You need 1/2 tsp of nutmeg.
  10. You need 1 1/2 tsp of vanilla extract.
  11. You need 1 tsp of baking powder.

No sugar added gluten free Blueberry carrot protein cake step by step

  1. Make the rice flour by using a coffee grinder or buy it at your local health food store.
  2. Mix all the dry ingredients separate from the wet ingredients except for the blueberries (save those for the last step).
  3. Preheat oven to 375Â°F.
  4. Coat any sized pan with the olive oil and the rice flour.
  5. Mix the dry and wet ingredients and pour in the pan.
  6. Sprinkle blueberries on top and put in the oven for 25-30 mins.
