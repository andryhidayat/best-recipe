Ingredients of Red Lentil Soup

  1. It's 1/2 cup of red lentils.
  2. You need 3 cups of vegetable broth.
  3. You need 4 oz of onion, 1/4” diced.
  4. You need 4 oz of carrot, 1/4” diced.
  5. Prepare 1 tbsp of minced garlic.
  6. Prepare 2 tbsp of tomato paste.
  7. It's 1 tsp of ground cumin.
  8. You need 1/8 tsp of cayenne pepper.
  9. Prepare 1/2 oz of fresh lemon.
  10. Prepare 1/2 tsp of tarragon.
  11. Prepare to taste of Salt.

Red Lentil Soup step by step

  1. Sweat onions and carrots in a pot with one tbsp oil, 2 oz water, and 1/2 tsp of salt. When onions are clarified, add lentils, broth, garlic, tomato paste, cumin and cayenne. Bring to a boil over med-high heat. Lower heat and simmer, uncovered, until vegetables are very tender and lentils begin to fall apart, about 20 minutes. Squeeze in lemon and stir before serving.
