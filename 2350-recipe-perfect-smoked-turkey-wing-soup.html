Smoked Turkey Wing Soup instructions

  1. Place wings in a large enough put too cover wings with water, cover with water, it was about 6 cups for me..
  2. Place on medium high heat.
  3. Smash garlic, add to pot.
  4. Clean ginger, add to pot.
  5. Slice hot pepper, add to pot.
  6. Bring to a boil, turn down to a simmer, cover, simmer for 2 hours..
  7. Turn off heat. Remove wings, set aside to cool..
  8. Remove jalapeno, garlic and ginger from broth.
  9. Pull, chop, or otherwise remove meat from wings. Discard skin if your significant other doesn't like it üòÅ.
  10. Put meat in broth, back on medium high heat.
  11. Add rice and lentils.
  12. Bring to a boil, turn down to a rapid simmer.
  13. Clean and cut green beans, add to pot.
  14. Clean and smash garlic, add to pot.
  15. Clean cut mushrooms, add to pot.
  16. Taste broth.
  17. Add black pepper, fish sauce and bullion until salt level you like is reached.
  18. About 5 minutes from rice being cooked, add spinach, stir in.
  19. Serve when done to your liking.
