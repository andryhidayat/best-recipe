Ingredients of My Chicken Salad

  1. Prepare 2 cans of Swanson white premium chicken breast in can.
  2. You need 1 cup of mayo.
  3. It's 2 tsp of granulated white sugar.
  4. Prepare 1 stalk of celery chopped.
  5. Prepare 1 tbsp of lemon juice.
  6. Prepare 1/2 cup of sweetened dried cranberries.
  7. It's 2 of green onions chopped.
  8. You need 1/2 tsp of white ground pepper.
  9. You need 1/4 tsp of ground mustard.
  10. It's 1/8 tsp of celery seed.
  11. Prepare 1/8 tsp of ground black pepper.
  12. You need 1/2 tsp of garlic powder.
  13. Prepare 1/2 tsp of onion powder.
