Short Adas (Creamy Lentil Soup) instructions

  1. Peel and chop onions roughly.
  2. Place in mill jar.
  3. Use pulse to mince them.
  4. Chop carrots roughly.
  5. Place in mill jar.
  6. Use pulse to chop them fine,stirring a couple of times.
  7. Heat the oil in a deep pan.
  8. Add garlic paste.
  9. Sauté till fragrant.
  10. Add chopped vegetables,lentil and rice.
  11. Add chicken stock,baharat and cumin powder.
  12. Cook till lentils are soft.
  13. Add salt.
  14. Cook till warm then blend in the blender jar,in two batches.
  15. Return to the cooking pan.
  16. Just before serving,heat the soup thoroughly and serve garnished with cilantro and croutons.
