Chicken Donne Biryani Recipe instructions

  1. Wash the chicken, apply turmeric and set aside. Now make green paste. In mixie jar add coriander, ginger,garlic, mint, green chillies, tomato, lemon juice and onion. Make fine paste.
  2. Apply this paste to chicken and marinate it for 30 mins or more. Wash the rice and soak it for 30 mins..
  3. In a pressure cooker add ghee/ oil 3 tbsp. once the oil is hot put all dry masalas (khada masalas). Now add 1 medium thinly sliced onion. Fry until brown..
  4. After that put in the chicken and curd. Give it a nice mix. Add salt. Mix nicely. Drain the water from rice and add it to the chicken.
  5. Mix that well. Pour required amount of water and pressure cook it for one whistle on low flame. Please check salt before you close the lid.
