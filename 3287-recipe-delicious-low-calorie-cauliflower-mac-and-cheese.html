Ingredients of low calorie cauliflower mac and cheese

  1. Prepare 8 cup of cauliflower florets.
  2. You need 2 tbsp of Earth balance or similar butter.
  3. You need 3 tbsp of whole wheat flour.
  4. Prepare 2 cup of reduced fat shredded cheddar.
  5. Prepare 1/2 cup of nutritional yeast.
  6. It's 1 dash of Cayenne pepper.
  7. It's 1 dash of onion powder.
  8. Prepare 1 dash of pepper.
  9. You need 2 of egg yolks.
  10. Prepare 1 1/2 cup of bread crumbs.
  11. It's 2 clove of minced garlic.
  12. You need 2 cup of unsweetened almond milk.

low calorie cauliflower mac and cheese instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F.
  2. Bring large pot of water to boil and season with salt.
  3. Add cauliflower and cook until tender.
  4. Drain, reserving 1 Cup of liquid and set aside.
  5. In same pot melt butter on medium heat.
  6. Whisk in flour, garlic, milk , cooking liquid. Whisk the entire time until thickened.
  7. Remove from heat and add cheese , yeast , cayenne pepper, and egg. Stir until melted.
  8. Add cauliflower.
  9. Spray a casserole dish with nonstick cooking spray and pour in mixture.
  10. Top with bread crumbs and onion powder and pepper.
  11. Bake for 30 minutes.
  12. *tip* u can spray breadcrumbs with cooking spray to ensure it becomes crisp and brown.
