Mint and Curd chicken step by step

  1. Firstly wash the chicken. Add salt and turmeric powder mix it and boil it on medium flame for 15 minutes..
  2. Mean while make onion and tomato paste separately..
  3. Make paste of mint leaves,coriander leaves,green chilli. And add lemon juice to it..
  4. When chicken gets boil. Heat oil in a wolk add all whole spices to it and fry for 2 minutes..
  5. Now add oinon paste and cook for 3 to 4 minutes. Then add tomato and cook for 5 minutes. Then add ginger garlic paste to it and cool for 2 to 3 minutes..
  6. Now take curd in a bowl whisk it add all dry spices and salt to it add very little water to it and hain whisk it properly. Add this paste cooked onion tomato paste. And mix well cook it for 3 to 4 minutes..
  7. Now add boiled chicken to it and mix properly. Cook it on medium flame by covering for 5 to 7 minute..
  8. Now add mint paste to it and again cook for 3 to 4 miniut..
  9. Mint and curd chicken is ready to serve. Garnish it with some mint leaves..
