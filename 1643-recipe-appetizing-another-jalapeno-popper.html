Ingredients of Another jalapeño popper

  1. Prepare 10 of very large jalapeño peppers.
  2. Prepare 12 ounces of cream cheese.
  3. You need to taste of salt.
  4. You need 8 ounces of shredded mozzarella cheese.
  5. It's 1/4 cup of parsley, chopped.

Another jalapeño popper instructions

  1. Cut the steam ends off. Take the seeds and ribs out of each jalapeño.
  2. Preheat oven 400° Fahrenheit.
  3. Have the cream cheese not to cold but cool. Mix with mozzarella cheese.
  4. Add parsley and mix well.
  5. I used a muffin pan after I stuffed each pepper so the stuffing stay inside as they baked. I filled the two empty holes wirh water..
  6. Bake till peppers are done. Every pepper bakes different. Its according to the thickness of pepper but I would say bake at least 30 minutes..
  7. Serve I hope you enjoy.
