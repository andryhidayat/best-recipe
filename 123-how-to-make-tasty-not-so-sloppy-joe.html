Not so sloppy Joe instructions

  1. Fry ground beef on medium high heat.
  2. Add seasoning.
  3. Dice onion add to beef.
  4. Cook until beef is brown and drain.
  5. Reduce heat to medium add brown sugar ketchup and mustard.
  6. Stir and simmer for min..
  7. Serve on bread bun or dinner roll.
