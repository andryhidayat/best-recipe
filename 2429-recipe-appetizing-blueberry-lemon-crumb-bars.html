Blueberry-Lemon Crumb Bars♡ instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 190° celcius (375 F). Put parchment paper over the griddle, also cover the sides!.
  2. With the convex side of a spoon, mix the lemon zest with 1 cup of sugar. Push it hard untill the sugar becomes a little moist..
  3. Mix the lemon-sugar-mix, 3 cups of flour and a teaspoon of baking powder. Then add the butter and the egg and mix with a fork. Do this untill there are no big butter parts left and everything is sort of equally mixed..
  4. Spread half of the mix over the baking plate. Push it with the convex side of a spoon equally over the plate..
  5. Mix the blueberries with half a cup of sugar, the cornstarch and the lemon juice..
  6. Spread the blueberry mix over the plate. Spread the rest of the dough over it. Do not push it, you want it to be crumbly!.
  7. Place in the oven for 45 minutes or untill the top get's brown. Let it cool down afterwards. Optionally you can make a frosting of powdered sugar, lemon juice and milk! Keep them in the fridge:) Enjoy!♡.
