Ingredients of Treat day Smoothie with Nutella

  1. Prepare 1/2 cup of Skimmed Milk.
  2. Prepare 1 of Apple.
  3. It's 5 of Strawberries.
  4. Prepare 1 of Banana.
  5. It's 3 tbsp of Greek Yoghurt.
  6. It's 1 tsp of Syrup or Honey.
  7. It's 2 tsp of Peanut Butter.
  8. It's 2 tsp of Nutella.
  9. It's 1 of Orange Juice.
  10. Prepare 4 of Ice cubes.
  11. It's 3 tbsp of Porridge Oats.

Treat day Smoothie with Nutella instructions

  1. Pour the milk into the blender..
  2. Peel and chop the Apple and banana . Chop the strawberries and add all fruit to the blender..
  3. Blend the fruit and milk with 4 ice cubes..
  4. Open blender and add in the Greek yoghurt, nutella, honey and peanut butter..
  5. Sprinkle in the porridge oats and blend..
  6. Serve immediately and enjoy!.
