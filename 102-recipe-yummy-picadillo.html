Ingredients of Picadillo

  1. You need 1 kg of ground beef/pork/chicken.
  2. You need 1 of onion, chopped.
  3. You need 1/2 clove of garlic, chopped.
  4. It's 1/4 tsp of oregano.
  5. It's 1 tbsp of fish sauce (optional).
  6. It's of cooking oil.
  7. You need of salt and black pepper.
  8. It's 1/2 cup of water/beef broth or dissolved beef buillon cube.
  9. Prepare 1 1/2 cups of tomato sauce.
  10. You need 1 tablespoon of tomato paste.
  11. Prepare 4 of large potatoes (cubed).
  12. Prepare 2 of carrots (cubed).
  13. Prepare 1/2 cup of raisins.
  14. It's 1 can of green peas.
  15. You need of Green pitted olives (optional).

Picadillo step by step

  1. Saute garlic and onion in oil. Set aside. Cook ground beef/pork/chicken in oil until brown..
  2. If you want potatoes to be a bit crunchy you may cook them in oil too before mixing it with cooked ground meat, carrots and green peas..
  3. Add water/beef broth and bring to boil. Add raisins and olives. Pour tomato sauce and tomato paste and simmer for about 15-20 minutes or until sauce reduces..
  4. Season with oregano, salt, fish sauce and black pepper. Best served with white rice or corn bread and fried plantain bananas..
