Ingredients of Fakes Soupa (Lentil Soup)

  1. It's 300 grams of Lentil boiled.
  2. You need 1 of Carrot chopped.
  3. Prepare 1 of Onion chopped.
  4. Prepare 1 of Potato chopped.
  5. You need 2 of whole Garlic cloves.
  6. You need 150 grams of Bacon cut into small pieces.
  7. You need 3 of Daphne Leaves/Bay Leaves.
  8. It's 2 teaspoons of Tomato Paste.
  9. It's 2 of Fresh tomatoes grated.
  10. You need 1 of Red Bell Pepper diced.
  11. You need of Salt.
  12. You need of Pepper.
  13. It's 1/2 cup of Olive Oil.

Fakes Soupa (Lentil Soup) step by step

  1. Boil Lentil for at least 15 minutes and drain. In a large soup pan, add about 4 tablespoons of Olive Oil and sauté the Bacon..
  2. Add in Onion, Carrot, Potato, Bell Pepper, Garlic, and stir..
  3. Toss in the lentil and continue to stir, so that all is nicely combined..
  4. Add water, grated tomatoes, tomato paste, salt, pepper, 1/2 cup Olive Oil and Bay leaves, bring to a boil and let it simmer on low heat for about 1 hour..
  5. I hope you enjoy this recipe, a bit of a twist from the usual Lentil Soup.
