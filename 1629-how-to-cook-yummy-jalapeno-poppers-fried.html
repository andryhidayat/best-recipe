Jalapeño Poppers, Fried instructions

  1. Heat oil.
  2. Cut the ends off the peppers where the stem is..
  3. Remove the seeds and the ribs inside each pepper.
  4. It's a lot of work try not to puncture the other part of the peppers..
  5. Add the herb and spices to the cream cheese.
  6. Mix well and add the chives oil..
  7. Pack the mixture into the peppers.
  8. Now about the last six, before you stuff them add the raspberry jam to the rest of the cream cheese.
  9. Mix well and stuff into the six peppers.
  10. Get the flour eggs and tempura spices listed and add to a mixing bowl.
  11. Add warm water slowly till thick but pourable batter.
  12. Coat the filled peppers and fry.
  13. Move to a paper towel to absorb excessive amounts of oil.
  14. Serve I made a sauce you can see my recipe called Raspberry Jalapeño Popper Sauce..
