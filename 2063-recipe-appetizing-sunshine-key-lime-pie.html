Ingredients of Sunshine Key Lime Pie

  1. Prepare 1 box of 8 oz - cream cheese.
  2. It's 2 of -6 oz- Key Lime Pie yogurt low fat.
  3. You need 1 packages of knox original gelatine unflavored.
  4. You need 1 tbsp of Fresh lime juice Squeezed.
  5. Prepare 1/2 cup of sugar.
  6. Prepare 1 of graham cracker pie crust.
  7. Prepare 1/4 cup of boiling water.
  8. Prepare 1/4 tsp of lime rind Grated and chopped fine.

Sunshine Key Lime Pie instructions

  1. with a electric mixer,in a mix bowl add cream cheese, and sugar mix until creamy..
  2. 1/4 cup hot boiling water mix gelatine.stir well let set..
  3. add yogurts to cream cheese mix. whip 1 minute, now add lime juice and lime rind..
  4. stir gelatine again, add to cream cheese mixture. whip on medium speed for 1 more minute..
  5. scrape cream cheese muxture around mixing bowl..
  6. whip one minute longer..
  7. pour pie mixture into pie crust, place in refrigerator for 3 hours..
  8. garnish pie how you like, ready to serve.enjoy..
