Mom's macaroni and cheese step by step

  1. Heat butter and oil in a skillet pan.
  2. Add onion,garlic and bell peppers.
  3. Stir ingredients until the onion is golden.
  4. Add chopped tomatoes and water.
  5. Let it stew for about 12 to 15 mins.
  6. Meanwhile in a big pan boil water.
  7. When water is boiled,add macaroni.
  8. Cook for 6 to 8 mins.
  9. For the sauce in the skillet, add the herbs,salt and pepper..
  10. Drain the pasta from the water Return the pasta to the pan.
  11. Add sauce,the cheddar cheese, and mix.
  12. Pour in a serving bowl.
  13. Add parmesan cheese to garnish.
  14. Serve and enjoy.
