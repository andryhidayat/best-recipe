Jalapeno Duck Poppers step by step

  1. After cleaning ducks, soak in salt water for 2-4 days. Change water out daily..
  2. Marinate duck breasts in Italian dressing for 2 days..
  3. Fill halfed jalapenos with cheddar and cream cheese mix..
  4. Top cheesy jalapeno with duck breast..
  5. Wrap with 2 pieces of bacon..
  6. Heat in glass pan for 45 minutes or till bacon is crispy on 375..
  7. Engage with a nice red wine blend. (optional).
