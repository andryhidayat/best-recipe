Jamaican jerk chicken wings instructions

  1. Take all dry ingredients for Jamaican seasoning and put in sealable container, seal out and shake..
  2. Take chicken wings and place them in a large enough container to hold them, pour olive oil on them then add Jamaican seasoning, run chicken until completely covered then place in fridge for about 4 hours or overnight (best prep day before so this would go by quicker)..
  3. When ready preheat oven at 350Â°.
  4. Take chicken out of fridge and place on aluminum foil covered banking sheet, evenly distributed..
  5. Cook for 35 minutes or when chicken starts pulling from the bone.
  6. Remove and enjoy.
