Ingredients of Yummy super Stuffed mushrooms

  1. Prepare 2 of red potatoes diced.
  2. It's 1/2 cup of peas.
  3. You need 1/2 lb of Italian sausage.
  4. It's 4 slices of Bacon.
  5. It's 4 of large mushroom caps.
  6. Prepare 1 of small onion chopped.
  7. It's 1/2 of a pepper chopped.
  8. Prepare 6 of baby carrots sliced.
  9. It's 2 cloves of garlic mashed and chopped.
  10. You need 2 tsp of cracked black pepper.
  11. Prepare 2 tsp of pink salt.
  12. It's 1.5 of tblsp chopped cilantro.
  13. Prepare 1/4 cup of mozzarella.
  14. You need 1/4 cup of cheddar.

Yummy super Stuffed mushrooms instructions

  1. Fry chopped bacon crispy.
  2. Fry sausage potato and carrots add onions peppers and garlic Cook till onions are translucent.
  3. Mix all cooked ingredients in one bowl add half the cheeses peas cilantro and seasoning mix well add season slowly to make sure it's is spread throughout.
  4. Fill mushrooms caps top with 1 tblsp of cheese bake till cheese is melted and mushrooms cooked 10 to 15 min on 350 watch carefully check every 5ish minutes.
