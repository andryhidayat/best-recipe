Ingredients of Banana Curd Smoothie

  1. Prepare 4 of Ripe Bananas.
  2. You need 2 Cup of Curd.
  3. It's 5 Tbspn of Honey.
  4. You need 1/2 Tsp of Lemon Juice.
  5. Prepare 1 Tsp of Vallina Essence.
  6. Prepare as needed of Mint Leaves For Garnishing.

Banana Curd Smoothie instructions

  1. Peel bananas put them in a blender jar..
  2. Add lemon juice, vanilla essence, honey, curd and blend till smooth..
  3. Serve chilled and garnished with mint leaves..
