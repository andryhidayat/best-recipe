King Cluck Turkey Burgers step by step

  1. Mix all the ingredients together until thoroughly combined..
  2. If you have time, chill mixture in the freezer for at leastÂ 1 hour. Chilling the mixture will firm it up, increase the flavor and make shaping patties easier. Go to next step..
  3. Form mixture into patties of desired size. If you keep them fairly thick, they will be moister..
  4. Pour olive oil into a non stick skillet (I use cast iron)on medium high. Heat oil until very hot but not scorching..
  5. Place patties in skillet without moving until bottom side is brown and crusted - about 5 minutes..
  6. Turn patties over, reduce heat to medium and cook 6 to 8 mins longer or until center is no longer pink and juices run clear..
