Chhole tikki with curd sauce instructions

  1. Take 1 cup choole. Soak it in water for 12 hours. Then grind it nicely..
  2. Now add white and green onion, garlic, green garlic, green chilli, meant and coriander leaves, salt, cumin powder, garam masala and chaat masala, bread crums, gratedcheese. Mixed all nicely. Now make small tikki..
  3. First put tikkis in refrigerator for 20 minutes. Then pour it in bread crums. And fry it on medium flame till it turns golden brown colour..
  4. Now make sauce. First take 1 tsp tal. Grind it nicely. In that add 1 tsp curd, 2 tsp mayonnaise, lamonjuce, methi kuriya, salt, mari powder garlic paste. Mix it..
  5. Now healthy tikkis are ready with healthy sauce. Serve it in lunch box..
