Ingredients of Memorial Day Rump roast on the grill

  1. You need 3 3/4 lb of rump roast cut into half inch slices.
  2. Prepare 1/4 cup of Cavenders Greek seasonings, I got mine at Walmart.
  3. You need 1 tsp of Adolfs meat tenderizer.
  4. Prepare 1 1/2 tsp of paprika.
  5. Prepare 1 tsp of granulated garlic powder.
  6. You need 1/4 cup of butter.
