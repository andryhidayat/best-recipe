Cheesy sausage ziti step by step

  1. Remove casing Brown ground Italian sausage add onions garlic peppers cook additional 3-5 minutes on med till fragrant.
  2. While cooking meat boil pasta 8- 10 minutes till done.
  3. Drain pasta add all ingredients setting aside 1/2 cup mozzarella stir well.
  4. Top with reserved mozzarella bake until cheeses melted and bubbled.
