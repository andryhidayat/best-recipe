Chicken Malai tikka step by step

  1. Firstly marinate the chicken pieces with 1 tsp of garlic 1 tsp of ginger and 1 tsp of green chillies and 1tsp salt for at least 30 mins (the more you marinate more it become tender and juicy).
  2. And all the ingredients in a bowl and then add keep for rest for 10 mins..
  3. Sew in a skewer and heat it either in a pan or in oven 10 mins and then flip it keep for another 10 mins (do not forget to preserve the moisture) don't overcook it..
