Ingredients of Ground Beef and Broccoli

  1. Prepare 1/4 cup of oil.
  2. It's 1 each of large onion chopped.
  3. You need 3 each of garlic cloves chopped.
  4. It's 1 1/2 lb of ground beef.
  5. It's 1 1/2 lb of frozen broccoli thawed.
  6. It's 2 each of small carrots shredded.
  7. Prepare 1 packages of Knorr Brown Sauce.
  8. It's 3 cup of water.
  9. Prepare 1 tsp of salt.
  10. It's 1/4 tsp of pepper.
  11. Prepare of Optional.
  12. Prepare 1 tbsp of Soy sauce.
  13. You need 1 of Sweet and sour sauce.

Ground Beef and Broccoli step by step

  1. Heat oil in pan.
  2. add onions, cook until tender.
  3. add garlic, stir, cook 1 min.
  4. add ground beef, stir fry until brown.
  5. add broccoli, stir fry 3 minutes.
  6. add carrots.
  7. add packet of Knorr brown sauce and stir.
  8. add water, salt and pepper.
  9. simmer 20 minutes on medium heat.
  10. stir in soy sauce.
  11. remove from heat, serve over rice.
