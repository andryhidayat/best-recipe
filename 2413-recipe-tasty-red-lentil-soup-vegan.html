Ingredients of Red lentil soup - vegan

  5. You need 1/2 of onion, peeled and chopped.

  12. Prepare of juice of half a lemon.
  13. It's to taste of salt and pepper.
  14. You need of Some chilli flakes and parsley leaves to garnish if you like.
