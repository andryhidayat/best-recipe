Ingredients of Key Lime Pie (Weight Watchers version).

  1. It's 1 of reduced fat graham cracker crust.
  2. It's 1 small pack of lime sugar free jello.
  3. Prepare 1/4 cup of boiling water.
  4. You need 2 small containers of yoplait light key lime yogurt.
  5. Prepare 1 tub (8 oz) of fat free cool whip.

Key Lime Pie (Weight Watchers version). step by step

  1. Dissolve the jello in the boiling water..
  2. Stir in yogurt with wire whisk..
  3. Fold in cool whip..
  4. Spread in crust and refrigerate for at least 2 hours..
  5. Enjoy!.
