Ingredients of Turkey & veggie chili

  14. Prepare of Olive oil.

  16. Prepare to taste of Shredded cheese.

  18. You need to taste of Sour cream.

Turkey & veggie chili step by step

  1. In a large pot, heat about 2-3 tbsp olive oil. Add onion and zucchini and cook until almost done..
  2. Add chili powder, cumin and garlic, stir well and cook for just a couple minutes to release flavors..
  3. Remove onion/zucchini mix and set aside. Add turkey to same pot and cook until browned..
  4. Add onion/zucchini mixture back to pot. Add tomatoes, black beans, kidney beans, corn, spinach, oregano and salt & pepper. Stir well..
  5. If chili doesn't look soupy enough (not enough liquid) add some chicken stock until desired consistency is reached. Note: be careful not to add too much as chili gets more soupy as it cooks..
  6. Bring to a boil, then turn heat down to low, cover and cook for 20 minutes..
  7. Top with shredded cheese, sour cream, avocado and tortilla chips..
  8. All done! Enjoy.
