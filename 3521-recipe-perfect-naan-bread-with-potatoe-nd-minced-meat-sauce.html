Naan bread with potatoe nd minced meat sauce instructions

  1. Sift flour with salt and baking powder. Make a hole add oil, garlic and oregano, mix very well..
  2. Add in warm milk and knead to a soft dough. The dough should not be sticky, cover and set aside for 15 mins..
  3. Divide dough into four golf ball size and roll thinly. Bake in a non stick pan and turn the other side and bake.note :cover wit kitchen towel wen done to avoid dryness of ur bread.

  5. Fry for some min. Add little water 2 cook d meat nd potato, add spices and cover to boil 4 some min..
