Ingredients of Lemon Magic Bars

  1. It's 1 of roll pillsbury refrigerated sugar cookies.
  2. It's 1 tsp of grated lemon peel.
  3. Prepare 1 1/2 cup of white vanilla baking chips.
  4. You need 3/4 cup of sweetened shredded coconut.
  5. You need 1/2 cup of chopped macadamia nuts, if desired.
  6. It's 1 can of fat-free sweetened condensed milk (not evaporated).
  7. You need 1 tbsp of lemon juice.
  8. You need 1/4 tsp of lemon extract, if desired.

Lemon Magic Bars instructions

  1. Heat oven to 350Â°F. Line 13x9 pan with foil and spray with cooking spray..
  2. In a large bowl, break up cookie dough. Add lemon peel; beat with electric mixer on low speed until peel well combined. Press dough into bottom of pan..
  3. Sprinkle white chips, coconut and macadamia nuts over dough..
  4. In a small bowl stir together condensed milk, lemon juice and lemon extract. Pour over top..
  5. Back 28-35 minutes or until edges are golden brown and center is slightly glossy. Cool completely, about 45 minutes..
  6. Cut into 6 rows by 4 rows..
