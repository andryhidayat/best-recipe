Ingredients of 60 Second softy choc chip cookie (in a mug)

  1. It's 1 tbsp of Butter (at room temp).
  2. You need 1 tbsp of White granulated sugar.
  3. Prepare 1 tbsp of Dark brown sugar.
  4. Prepare 1/4 tsp of Vanilla extract.
  5. You need 1 of Egg yolk (do not use the whites !).
  6. It's 3 tbsp of All-purpose flour.
  7. You need pinch of Of salt.
  8. You need 2 tbsp of chocolate chips.
  9. It's 1 of A handful of mixed chopped nuts (optional).

60 Second softy choc chip cookie (in a mug) step by step

  1. Either in your microwavable mug that the cookie will be cooked in or a separate small bowl, add the room temp butter.
  2. Add the white sugar and brown sugar to the butter and mix together.
  3. Add your egg yolk and vanilla.
  4. Now add the dry ingredients. The flour, salt and chocolate chips, and nuts if you wish.
  5. Combine all ingredients together.
  6. Now microwave for approximately 60, depending on your microwave watts. My microwave is 800w so it takes roughly a minute, maybe a little longer.
  7. Play around with the times to get it perfect. Remember not to over cook your cookie by leaving it in for too long.
  8. Once cooked, add topping of your choice. Cold icecream and chocolate sauce with a sprinkle of nuts tastes really good :).
