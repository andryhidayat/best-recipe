Ingredients of Macaroni Salad With Peanuts

  1. It's 1 Cup of Macaroni.
  2. It's 1 Tsp of Salt.
  3. Prepare 1 Tbsp of Olive Oil.
  4. You need 1/2 Cup of Bell Peppers All 3 colours.
  5. Prepare 1/2 Cup of Roasted Peanuts.
  6. You need 1 tsp of Mixed Herbs Oregano, Basil, Parsley, Chilli Flakes.
  7. You need 1 tsp of Vinegar.

Macaroni Salad With Peanuts instructions

  1. Chop bell peppers in small pieces. Roast peanuts..
  2. Take 3 Cups water in a big pan and boil it. When the water starts boiling, add salt and Macaroni. Stir it so that it doesn’t stick to the bottom of the pan. Boil for 7 to 8 minutes till tender. Drain in a strainer and keep it aside..
  3. In a wok, take olive oil and saute bell peppers. When slightly soft, mix in the boiled macaroni, herbs and roasted peanuts. Adjust salt. Toss everything. Lastly, add vinegar and give a final toss..
  4. Serve with love, soup optional..
