Cilantro-lemon chutney instructions

  1. Grind the corriander leaves, coconut, curd, black pepper and the juice of lemon together in a mixer until it becomes a paste.
  2. Heat oil in a pan and crackle the mustard seeds and add it to the chutney..
  3. Chutney is ready..
