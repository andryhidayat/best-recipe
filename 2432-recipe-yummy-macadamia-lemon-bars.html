Macadamia Lemon Bars instructions

  1. This recipe is from Taste of Home Magazine..
  2. Preheat oven to 350Â°..
  3. Large bowl- mix flour, confectioners sugar, sugar, and melted butter until crumbly. Stir in nuts..
  4. Press into bottom and 1/2 inch up sides of greased 8x8 square baking dish. Bake 15-20 min. or until light brown..
  5. Meanwhile, for filling....small bowl- whisk sugar, flour, baking powder, and salt..
  6. Beat in eggs, lemon juice, and lemon peel until blended..
  7. Pour filling mixture over hot crust. Sprinkle with nuts. Bake 10-15 min. or until lightly browned..
  8. Cool completely. Cut into bars. Sprinkle with confectioners sugar..
