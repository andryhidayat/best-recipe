Lemon chicken instructions

  1. Wash the chicken pieces. Add curd,lemon juice(2 spoon),turmeric powder,red chilli powder and salt. Mix it well and refrigerate it for 2 hour for marination..
  2. Grind the onions and tomato separately yo make a fine paste..
  3. Boil poppy seeds melon seeds and cashew in little water for 5 minute. After cooling grind it to make fine paste..
  4. In a pan heat oil. Add bay leaf,red chilli, black pepper,clove and cumin seeds. Fry for 2 minits.
  5. Add onion paste and fry for 3 to 4 minutes. And now add ginger garlic paste and fry for 2 to 3 minutes..
  6. Now add paste of cashew,melon and poppy seeds to it. And cook for 3 ro 4 minutes..
  7. Now add toamto pury and cook it for 4 to 5 minute..
  8. Now add all dry spices and cook for 3 to 4 minute..
  9. Now add marinated chicken, sakta and mix well. Cook it for to minutes on low flame by covering..
  10. Now add 1 cup of water mix well and hain cook for 5 to 7 minutes on medium flame..
  11. Now put off the game add 2 to 3 spoon of lemon juice and coriander leaf. Lemon chicken is ready. Serve it with tandoori roti or palne roti..
