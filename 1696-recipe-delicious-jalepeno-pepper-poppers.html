Ingredients of jalepeno pepper poppers

  1. You need 15 each of whole jalepeno peppers.
  2. Prepare 2 packages of honey nut cream cheese (not the whipped kind).
  3. It's 1 packages of center cut bacon.

This recipe for fried jalapeño poppers is perfect for a party. The stuffing for the peppers is made with a combination of cream cheese and cheddar. Jalapeno Poppers have to be one of my favorite snacks. Jalapeños Peppers stuffed with cheese and bacon make a great appetizer for a crowd!

jalepeno pepper poppers instructions

  1. Cut the tops off the peppers and slice them in half lengthwise. Seed the jalepenos with a spoon..
  2. Preheat oven to 350..
  3. Spoon in the honey nut cream cheese into each pepper. (It works with any kind of cream cheese, but the honey nut I've found tastes the best.).
  4. Slice the bacon in half and use each one to wrap around each jalepeno..
  5. Put the pepper poppers in the oven until the bacon is to your liking. I prefer my bacon is not cooked too much so 10-15 minutes works for me, but you can make it as crispy as you like it, it won't change the effect of the peppers..
  6. Enjoy :) Recipe makes 30 peppers total..
