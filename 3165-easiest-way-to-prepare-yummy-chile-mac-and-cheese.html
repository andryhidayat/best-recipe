Chile Mac and Cheese step by step

  1. Boil elbows until al dente.
  2. Put flour and butter in a pot and cook into a blonde roux.
  3. Add milk and cheese to roux add salt, pepper, garlic powder and onion powder.
  4. Then whisk until béchamel consistently and add chillies simmer on low for 5 min.
  5. Toss elbows in the cheese sauce then transfer into rectangular baking pan.
  6. Top with shredded Colby jack and seasoned Panko breadcrumbs..
  7. Bake 375 degrees for 20 min until golden brown.
  8. Let cool a few minutes before serving and Enjoy.
