Ingredients of Quick and easy red lentil soup

  1. Prepare 1 1/4 l of home-made ham stock.
  2. It's 1 of carrot, diced small.
  3. You need 1 stick of celery, chopped small.
  4. It's 1 of onion, chopped small.
  5. You need 175 g of red lentils.
  6. It's 1 of ham stock cube.
  7. Prepare A few of small bits of ham, if you have any spare (optional).
  8. Prepare of Ground black pepper.

Quick and easy red lentil soup step by step

  1. Put all the ingredients except the pepper in a saucepan and bring to the boil, stirring to avoid sticking..
  2. Reduce to a simmer and cook for 20 minutes, stirring occasionally..
  3. Taste and season with pepper if wished. Salt should not be necessary with home-made ham stock..
  4. Serve piping hot with granary bread, crusty rolls or whatever you fancy!.
