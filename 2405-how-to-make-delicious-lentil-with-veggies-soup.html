Lentil with Veggies soup step by step

  2. Take a pressure cooker put all ingrediente into cooker allow to cook 3 whistles..
  3. Then blend nicely pour into strainer will get fine semi liquid.
  4. Heat a pan put butter add chopped garlic,bay leaf fry for 1min then pour already prepared Veggies liquid add salt,pepper keep boiling, once it get thick and bubbling turn off..
  5. Take a serving bowl pour lentil soup garnish with fresh cream, coriander,bread croutons and it's ready to serve.
