Ingredients of Game Day Deviled Eggs

  1. It's 12 of Hard Boiled Eggs, shells removed and cut in half and yolks removed to a bowl.Whites reserved..
  2. It's 1/3 cup of mayonnaise.
  3. Prepare 2 tsp of dijon mustard, or mustard you like.
  4. It's 2 tbsp of minced cooked ham, I used black forrest ham.
  5. It's 1 tsp of lemon juice.
  6. Prepare 1 tbsp of minced celery.
  7. You need 1 tsp of hot sauce, I used franks red hot.
  8. You need 1/2 tsp of black pepper.
  9. It's 1 dash of salt, to taste depending how salty your ham is.
  10. It's 1 bunch of chives.
