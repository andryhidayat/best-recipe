Ingredients of Ham And Egg With Cheese Fried Rice

  1. Prepare 2 cup of cooked leftover rice.
  2. It's 2 of eggs.
  3. It's 1 tsp of light soy sauce.
  4. Prepare 1 cup of thinly cured ham.
  5. You need 1 1/2 tbsp of butter.
  6. It's of SEASONING.
  7. It's 1 dash of spicy cajun spice seasoning.
  8. It's 1 dash of Italian mix herbs.
  9. Prepare of GARNISH OPTION CRISPY MINT.
  10. It's 3 tbsp of oil.
  11. It's 6 of mint leaves.
  12. Prepare of GARNISH OPTION CHEESY.
  13. You need 1 tbsp of grated parmesan cheese.

Ham And Egg With Cheese Fried Rice step by step

  1. BAKE HAM.
  2. bake ham at 180 C for 10 minute till lightly brown and crispy then set aside.
  3. FRIED RICE.
  4. in pan with melted butter put beaten eggs till almost set then add rice and light soy sauce.
  5. fold eggs and rice gently then add seasoning and mix well then off heat.
  6. with oil in pan in medium heat then quick deep fry mint leave for 3 second till mint curl but not burn and brown.
  7. Serve ham and eggs fried rice with (CRISPY MINT inspired by jamie Oliver ).
