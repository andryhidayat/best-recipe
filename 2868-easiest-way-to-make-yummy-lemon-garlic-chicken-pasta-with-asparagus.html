Lemon Garlic Chicken Pasta with Asparagus instructions

  1. Cook pasta to desired texture.
  2. In a large skillet with a tbsp of olive oil cook chicken until brown on all sides.
  3. Add tomatoes asparagus, and parsley, turn heat to low, stir occasionally..
  4. For dressing: whisk together 2 tbsp olive oil, juice of 1 lemon, 1 minced garlic clove, salt and pepper..
  5. Mix chicken and veggies with pasta, pour dressing over everything, mix, top with grated parmesan and serve..
