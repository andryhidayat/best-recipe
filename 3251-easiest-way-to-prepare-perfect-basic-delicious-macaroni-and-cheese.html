Ingredients of Basic Delicious Macaroni And Cheese

  1. Prepare 1 box of Macaroni pasta.
  2. Prepare 2 of Eggs.
  3. It's 2 of large Block of sharp Cheddar cheese (add more cheese if wanted).
  4. Prepare 1/4 cup of Milk.
  5. It's 1 of Salt and pepper.

Basic Delicious Macaroni And Cheese instructions

  1. Preheat oven 350°F.
  2. Cook pasta. While that's cooking shred the cheese..
  3. Drain pasta. Add salt and pepper then add milk first (also cools down pasta).
  4. Beat the egg then add to pasta. Combine all ingredients by stirring.
  5. Now add the shredded cheese. Save some for the top.
  6. Put in oven about 20-25 mins or until the cheese is melted with a nice color at the top.
