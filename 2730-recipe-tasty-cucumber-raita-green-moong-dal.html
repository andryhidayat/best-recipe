Cucumber Raita Green moong Dal instructions

  1. Whisk the curd, add all the ingredients in it. Mix well. Transfer it into serving bowls garnish with coriander leaves, red chilli powder and cumin powder. Serve it cool..
  2. Heat ghee in a pan,add cumin seeds in it, when it splutter add green chilli, ginger, garlic.Soute it for minute on medium flame..
  3. Add tomato puree and all dry masala,mix it well. Cook till tadka released ghee..
  4. Now add boiled dal in it mix well.Add water to maintain consistancy. Simmer it for 2 -3minutes on medium flame. Turn off the flame. Add lemon juice and green coriander leaves..
