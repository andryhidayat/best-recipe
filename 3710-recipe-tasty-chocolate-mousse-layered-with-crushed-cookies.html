Chocolate Mousse Layered with Crushed Cookies step by step

  1. Separate the egg yolks from the whites..
  2. Whisk the whites with half the sugar (90 g) until they stand in peaks.
  3. Mix the butter and the chocolate in pieaces and melt it in the microwave. Then mix them well..
  4. Whisk the rest of the sugar with the yolks until they loook creamy..
  5. Mix the melted chocolate with the yolks..
  6. Add the previous mixture to the whites carefully not to destroy the peaks..
  7. Crush the cookies thinnely..
  8. In a proper recipient alternate layers of Mousse and Crushed Cookies..
  9. Take it to the fridge for a couple of hours and eat whenever you are ready..
