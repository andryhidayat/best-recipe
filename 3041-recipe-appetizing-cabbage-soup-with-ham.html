  9. You need 1 of large head Cabbage, bite size chopped.

Cabbage Soup with Ham step by step

  1. Soften onion in large pot with alittle fat.
  2. Add remaining ingredients except cabbage. Bring to boil..
  3. Lower heat, boil gently until vegs. soften (about 1 hr.)..
  4. Add cabbage. Boil gently until soft. About 1 hr..
  5. Serve with crusty buttered bread..
