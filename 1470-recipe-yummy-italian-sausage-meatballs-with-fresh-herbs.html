Ingredients of Italian Sausage Meatballs with Fresh Herbs

  1. It's 1 lb of ground beef (or bison).
  2. You need 1 lb of sweet Italian sausage.
  3. You need 2 clove of garlic, minced.
  4. It's 1 of sprig fresh rosemary, minced.
  5. It's 3 of sprigs fresh thyme, minced.
  6. It's 1 of long sprig fresh oregano, minced.
  7. You need 1/4 cup of roughly chopped flat leaf parsley.
  8. Prepare 1/2 small of yellow onion, roughly chopped.
  9. You need 1/2 cup of almond meal.
  10. It's 2 of eggs, whisked.
  11. You need 1 tsp of red pepper flakes.
  12. It's 1 of Black pepper.
  13. It's 1/4 cup of cream (optional).
  14. Prepare 1/2 cup of finely shredded parmesan (optional).
  15. It's 1/4 cup of bacon fat.

Italian Sausage Meatballs with Fresh Herbs step by step

  1. Remove the Italian sausage from its casing. Mix all of the ingredients, except bacon fat, together until well combined. With lightly oiled hands, roll the meatballs into the desired size..
  2. To cook, heat bacon fat in a saute pan over medium to medium-high heat. Once hot, add the meatballs. Fry 5–7 minutes, until bottom is browned..
  3. Turn meatballs to opposite side and fry until that side is nicely browned, another 5–7 minutes..
  4. Cut one open to determine if it is fully cooked on the inside. If not quite done, turn heat to low and cover pan for a few more minutes or put meatballs in a warm oven while you fry another batch..
