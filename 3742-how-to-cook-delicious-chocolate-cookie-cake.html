Chocolate Cookie Cake step by step

  1. Break the 300 grams of cookies into very small pieces.
  2. Mix the eggs and the sugar.
  3. Melt the butter in the microwave.
  4. Mix the butter with the eggs and sugar.
  5. Mix the cocoa powder with it.
  6. Add the cookies and mix them with a large spoon in the eggs, sugar, butter and cocoa.
  7. Take a cake mold put a plastic bag in it or use a silicone cake mold.
  8. Put the mixture in the mold and put it in the refrigerator for a night or day..
