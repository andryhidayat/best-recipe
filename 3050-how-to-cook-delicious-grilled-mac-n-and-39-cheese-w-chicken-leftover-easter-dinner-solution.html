Grilled Mac n' Cheese w/ Chicken (leftover Easter dinner solution) step by step

  1. Cut up meat into small pieces..
  2. Butter 1 side of each slice of bread..
  3. Place 1 slice of bread butter side down on greased skillet on medium/low heat (I use heat setting 3).
  4. Add chicken and Mac n cheese. Sprinkle with salt and pepper if desired..
  5. Place other slice of bread with butter side facing up on sandwich..
  6. Cook first side until it is dark (I wait until I hear sizzling) usually takes about 10-12 mins.
  7. Flip carefully so filling doesn't fall out and cook other side until golden brown..
  8. Enjoy!!.
