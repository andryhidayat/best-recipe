Sloppy joe tatertot casserole step by step

  1. Preheat the oven to º450.
  2. Brown hamburger meat.
  3. Add sauce and pepper flakes.
  4. When done, place meat in casserole dish.
  5. Add a dash of cheese to the top of the meat.
  6. Place frozen tatertots over meat..
  7. Smother it with shredded cheese.
  8. Place in oven for 25 minutes.
  9. Take out and let cool for 10 minutes.
  10. Enjoy!!.
