Chatpata Daliya Kabab step by step

  1. Beetroot, green chilli and ginger grind in the mixer jar..
  2. Put it in a bowl. Sprouted moong and Fenugreek Seeds (methi) grind coarsely and add in the chilli mixture bowl..
  3. Daliya grind separately and grind coarsely and carefully. (Do make the Paste).
  4. Ghee put in a pan when becomes ghee melted add gram flour and roasted on low flame..
  5. When gram flour roasted add chilli mixture and moong mixture in it and Mix them..
  6. Now add Daliya, mix them. Now add Salt, Red Chilli powder, Cumin powder, Lemon juice and mix well.After 2 to 3 minutes, mixture is ready. Switch off the gas..
  7. When mixture cool down. Make the balls and put a spoon of ghee in a pan. Roast the all kababs..
  8. When kababs are roasted. Put them in a plate..
  9. For Lemon Dip In same pan, add lemon juice,sugar, black Salt, Chilli flakes, mint leaves and roasted cumin seeds in it and mix well on low flame. Within 2 minutes dip is ready..
  10. Now kabab put in the lemon dip and rolled them gently. Kababs ready for serving..
  11. For Hung Curd All ingredients add in a bowl and mix well..
  12. For Mango Corn Salsa Mix all ingredients in a bowl..
  13. Kababs served with Chutney..
  14. Enjoy.
