  2. It's 200 grams of spam or ham or any meat of your preferences.

Spaghetti With Soup instructions

  1. Cut vegetables to big chunk and add all vegetable with water to a pot bring to a boil then simmer for 10 minute.
  2. Add spam and slice ham together with spaghetti and simmer for another 11 minute.
  3. Season with black pepper and serve immediately.
