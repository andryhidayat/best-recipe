Rajma chawal instructions

  1. Wash the soaked rajma in water. In a pressure cooker add rajma 4.5 cup water, 1 onion roughly chopped, 1 tomato roughly chopped, 1/2 tsp salt and 1/4 tsp turmeric powder. Pressure cook it for 15- 20 minutes or till rajma perfectly cooked..
  2. Heat oil in a large pan. Add cumin seeds when it turns brown add ground masala in it. Roast it on low medium flame. Stir continuously. When masala begins to releasing oil add curd and all the dry masala in it. Cook it..
  3. Now add boiled rajma in it. Add rajma stock to make gravy.Mix all very well. Cook it on low flame till it slightly thickens..
  4. Add crushed kasuri methi and lemon juice. Turn off the flame..
  5. Serve it with rice..
