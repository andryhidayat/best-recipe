Cookies Loaded Salad instructions

  1. Boil your eggs for 15 mins..
  2. Shred your lettuce in a bowl or pan. Add, cheese, tomatoes, onion,cucumber, meat, peppers, bacon bits, olives and croutons..
  3. Run cold water over your eggs and peel them. Place those aside to cool..
  4. Put the minced garlic and pickles on the salad. Cut the egg to your desire. Sprinkle salt and pepper across the salad to season..
