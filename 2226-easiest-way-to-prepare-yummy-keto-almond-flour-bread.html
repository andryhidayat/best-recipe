Ingredients of Keto Almond Flour Bread

  1. You need 3/4 cup of almond flour.
  2. It's 1/2 cup of flax meal.
  3. It's 3 tbsp of psyllium husk powder.
  4. It's 2 tsp of baking powder.
  5. Prepare Pinch of salt.
  6. You need 2 of Eggs.
  7. Prepare 2 tsp of apple cider vinegar.
  8. It's 1 cup of boiling water.

Keto Almond Flour Bread instructions

  2. Add eggs, vinegar and water. Mix..
  3. Place in baking dish. Shape as desired- bread will retain shape..
  4. Bake at 400 degrees for 50 minutes..
