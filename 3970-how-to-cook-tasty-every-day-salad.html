Every Day Salad instructions

  1. Chop vegetables and add to large bowl..
  2. Fry strips of tofu in a pan with some olive oil until both sides are golden..
  3. Remove tofu from pan and cut into bite size pieces..
  4. Mix vegetables in large bowl and serve. Top salad with tofu, raspberries and a fruity vinaigrette..
