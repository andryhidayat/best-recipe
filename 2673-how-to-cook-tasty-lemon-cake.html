Ingredients of Lemon cake

  1. You need 1.5 cup of flour.
  2. Prepare 1/2 tsp of baking soda.
  3. Prepare 1 tsp of baking powder.
  4. You need 2 tsp of lemon zest.
  5. You need 3/4 cup of vegetable oil.
  6. It's 1.5 cup of sugar.
  7. It's 1 cup of curd.
  8. It's 1/2 tsp of vanilla essence.
  9. You need 2 tbsp of lemon juice.
  10. It's 1/2 cup of milk + 1/2 tsp vinegar rested for 5 min(buttermilk).

Lemon cake instructions

  1. Preheat the oven at 180 degrees..
  2. Seive together flour, baking soda and baking powder. Add lemon zest in it..
  3. In a separate bowl, beat vegetable oil and sugar. Then beat in curd, vanilla essence and lemon juice..
  4. Mix dry ingredients and wet ingredients maintaining the consistency with the buttermilk made. Use a spatula to do it.. Don't overmix.
  5. Transfer the batter into a greased tin and bake it in a preheated oven at 180 degrees for about 35 minutes ðŸ˜Š.
  6. Demould when it cools. Dust icing sugar on it..
  7. Cut into slices and serve with glazed cherries and lemon zest.
