Ingredients of Key Lime Pie Chia Pudding

  1. You need 1 tin of light unsweetened coconut milk.
  2. Prepare 2 of limes, zested and juiced.
  3. Prepare 3/4 cup of chia seeds.
  4. It's Handful of chopped spinach.
  5. Prepare 1 tsp of maple syrup + more to taste.
  6. Prepare of Optional tsp spirulina or moringa for added nutrients.
  7. It's of Toppings: Mango or other tropical fruit, berries, toasted coconut flakes, granola or nuts.

Key Lime Pie Chia Pudding step by step

  1. Blend the spinach, maple syrup, lime juice and coconut milk (and spirulina) until smooth..
  2. Mix with chia seeds in a large bowl and refrigerate, splitting into 4 individual jars if desired..
  3. Chill for at least 2 hours, ideally overnight..
  4. Serve with desired toppings..
