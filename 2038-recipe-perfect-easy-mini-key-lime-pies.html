Ingredients of Easy Mini Key Lime Pies

  1. Prepare 4 of egg yolks.
  2. It's 2 of 14 oz cans of sweetened Condensed milk.
  3. You need 1 cup of lime juice.
  4. You need 12 of mini graham cracker pie crusts.

For black bottom Key lime pies, substitute Famous™ Chocolate Wafers, crushed, for the graham cracker crumbs. Don't feel like making whipped cream? Cool Whip™ whipped topping works great in a pinch. Just top the pies with it, and sprinkle with the lime peel.

Easy Mini Key Lime Pies step by step

  1. Preheat oven to 375° F..
  2. Combine egg yolks, condensed milk, and lime juice. Mix well..
  3. Pour into unbaked pie crusts..
  4. Bake in oven for 13 - 15 minutes..
  5. Allow to cool. Then garnish..
