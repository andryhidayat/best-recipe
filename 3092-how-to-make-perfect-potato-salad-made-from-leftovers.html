Ingredients of Potato Salad Made from Leftovers

  1. Prepare 2 of Potatoes.
  2. You need 2 of leaves Lettuce.
  3. Prepare 2 slice of Ham.
  4. Prepare 2 tbsp of Mayonnaise.
  5. Prepare 1 tsp of Whole-grain mustard.
  6. It's 1/2 tsp of Vinegar.
  7. It's 1 dash of Salt.
  8. It's 1 of Black pepper.

Potato Salad Made from Leftovers step by step

  1. Boil the potatoes. Once cooled, peel them and dice them into 1 - 1.5m cubes. Wash the lettuce leaves, chill them well, then shred..
  2. Slice the ham into 1 cm cubes. Put all the ingredients in a bowl and mix well. Season with salt and pepper, and mix in a little vinegar..
