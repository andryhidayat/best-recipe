Ingredients of Fried rice with fruit

  1. Prepare of fried Rice.
  2. Prepare 1 large of minneola tangelo.
  3. You need 2 2/3 oz of raisins.
  4. Prepare 8 oz of bean sprouts.
  5. You need 1 tbsp of citrus ponzu.
  6. You need 1/2 cup of coconut flakes.
  7. Prepare 4 cup of leftover jasmine rice refrigerated overnight.
  8. It's 1/4 cup of lite soy sauce.
  9. Prepare 2 tbsp of sugar.
  10. It's 2 tsp of olive oil, extra virgin.
  11. Prepare of garnish.
  12. You need 1/4 cup of pistachios.

Fried rice with fruit instructions

  1. Toast coconut flakes in a wok and heat raisins.
  2. Remove set aside add oil to wok add rice breaking up amy clumps.
  3. Stir fry till loose add ponzu and soya sauce stir well peel minneola add sugar.
  4. Add bean sprouts raisins and coconut stir well.
  5. Part out the plugs of minneola and cut them in half add to rice stirring constantly after cooking 5 minutes remove from heat dish out portions.
  6. Add the roughly chopped pistachios for garnish.
