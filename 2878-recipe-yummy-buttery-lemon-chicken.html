Buttery lemon chicken step by step

  1. Boil chicken leave a little under cooked.
  2. Cut chicken to about 1/2 inch slices sprinkle ms dash and Italian seasoning.
  3. Cut your lemon put lemon juices and 1 tsp of butter into pot bring to boil* you can add more lemon depending on your preference I add a little more from the 2nd slice.
  4. Add chicken then * add splenda completely optional * dont make sweet just counters some of the bitterness add rest of butter.
  5. Add 1/4 cup of water boil till fully cooked add salt pepper to flavor.
