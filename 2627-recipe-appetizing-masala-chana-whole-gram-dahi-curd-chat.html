Ingredients of Masala Chana Whole Gram Dahi Curd Chat

  1. Prepare of Masala Chana (whole gram).
  2. You need 2 cup of whole gram (soak overnight).
  3. Prepare 2 of Onions finely chopped.
  4. Prepare 2 of Tomatoes finely chopped.
  5. You need 2 tbsp of green chilli garlic ginger paste.
  6. Prepare 3 tbsp of oil.
  7. Prepare 1/2 tsp of mustard seeds.
  8. It's 1/4 tsp of asafoetida.
  9. It's 1 tsp of red chilli powder.
  10. You need 1/4 tsp of Turmeric Powder.
  11. Prepare 1 tsp of coriandor powder.
  12. Prepare 1/4 tsp of garam masala.
  13. Prepare to taste of Salt.
  14. Prepare 1/2 cup of water.
  15. You need of For Chat.
  16. You need 1 tsp of Garam Masala.
  17. You need 1/2 cup of curd.
  18. You need 1/4 cup of green spicy chutney.
  19. You need 1/4 cup of termarind dates chutney.
  20. You need 1/2 tsp of Chat Masala.
  21. It's 1 of Onion chopped.
  22. Prepare 1 of tomato chopped.
  23. It's 1/4 of raw mango chopped.
  24. You need 1 tsp of lemon juice.
  25. Prepare 1/2 cup of Bhel Sev homemade.
  26. It's Handful of coriander leaves.

Masala Chana Whole Gram Dahi Curd Chat instructions

  1. Get all masala whole gram ingredients..
  2. Take a cooker on Medium flame. Add oil, mustard seeds. Let crackled add asafoetida, onions. Saute it till golden..
  3. Add chopped tomatoes saute it. Add green chilli garlic ginger paste. Saute it for a min. Add red chilli powder, turmeric powder..
  4. Along with coriandor powder, garam masala, salt. Saute it. Add water..
  5. Now add Soaked gram. Mix it. Close the cooker lid get 3-4 whistle. Masala gram is ready..
  6. All all ingredients for masala gram chat is ready. Take Masala gram in plate. Spread Sev, Chopped onions, Tomatoes..
  7. Drizzle termarind-dates chutney, green spicy chutney and curd..
  8. Sprinkle chat masala, spread some more Sev, coriandor leaves. Squeeze lemon and serve e it..
