Ja'far salad #ramadan step by step

  1. Whisk together the dressing ingredients and allow to stand until the chia seeds start to thicken the dressing..
  2. Place the kale in a large serving bowl. Add half of the dressing and massage it into the kale - the longer one massages it, the better it will taste. It's very therapeutic!.
  3. Add the quinoa, raisins, almonds, black beans, carrot, cheese or chicken, chick peas and herbs along with the remaining dressing..
  4. Sprinkle with the seeds and a grind of pepper..
