Slow cooker italian sausage sandwiches step by step

  1. Add 2 tbsp olive oil to bottom of slow cooker.
  2. Add bag of frozen peppers & onions. Add italian seasoning to taste..
  3. Add sausage links.
  4. Cover and cook on low 8 hours. Stir occasionally if you can but if not it'll be fine..
  5. 1 hour prior to them being done add jar of marinara sauce..
  6. Serve on soft roll or over pasta. I add a slice of provolone before the sausage so it melts and then sprinkle with shaker cheese on top..
