Ingredients of Great Mac and Cheese

  1. Prepare 8 oz of Macaroni.
  2. You need 3 cup of milk.
  3. It's 1 tsp of salt.
  4. You need 1 1/2 tbsp of all-purpose flour.
  5. It's 1 cup of chopped onion.
  6. You need 1/4 cup of butter.
  7. Prepare 1 1/2 cup of shredded Cheddar cheese.

Great Mac and Cheese step by step

  1. Melt butter or margarine in a saucepan over medium heat. Add onions and saute. Stir in flour and salt..
  2. Add milk and macaroni to saucepan, and bring to a boil. Reduce heat, and cover. Simmer for 15 minutes or until pasta is tender, stirring occasionally..
  3. Add cheese and stir until cheese melts. Serve..
