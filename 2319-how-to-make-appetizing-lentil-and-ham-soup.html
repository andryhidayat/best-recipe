Ingredients of Lentil and Ham soup

  1. You need 1 1/2 cup of dried lentil.
  2. It's 1 cup of cooked diced ham.
  3. You need 1 cup of diced carrots.
  4. You need 1 cup of diced celery.
  5. Prepare 8 of tsps of tomato sauce.
  6. Prepare 1 cup of water.
  7. You need 1 quart of chicken broth (low sodium).
  8. It's 1 of bay leaf.
  9. It's to taste of onion powder.
  10. You need of pepper to taste1/2 tsp oregano.

Lentil and Ham soup step by step

  1. Mix lentil, carrots, celery and ham in large pot. Season with onion powder, oregano, pepper, and bay leaf. Stir over medium heat for 3-5 minutes..
  2. Stir in water, broth, and tomato sauce. Cover and simmer on low heat for 2 hours or until lentils are tender..
  3. Remove bay leaf before serving. Serve immediately with bread..
  4. Enjoy!.
