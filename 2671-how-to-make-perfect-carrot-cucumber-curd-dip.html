Ingredients of Carrot Cucumber Curd Dip

  1. Prepare 1/2 cup of Fresh hung curd.
  2. Prepare 1/2 cup of Thinly grated carrot.
  3. It's 1/2 cup of Thinly grated cucumber.
  4. It's 1/2 cup of Finely chopped onion.
  5. Prepare 1/4 tsp of Garlic paste.
  6. Prepare 1/2 of Finely chopped green chilli.
  7. You need 7-8 of Chopped mint leaves.
  8. You need 1 tbsp of Chopped coriander leaves.
  9. Prepare 1/2 tsp of Black pepper powder.
  10. It's to taste of Salt.
  11. You need 1 tsp of Lemon juice.

Carrot Cucumber Curd Dip step by step

  1. Squeeze out the water from the cucumber and discard it..
  2. Conbine all the ingredients in a deep bowl and mix well..
  3. Refrigerate for at least 1 hour..
  4. Serve chilled with biscuits toast or any snacks..
