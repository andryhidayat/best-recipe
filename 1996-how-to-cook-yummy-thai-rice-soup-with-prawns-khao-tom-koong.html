Ingredients of Thai Rice soup with Prawns / Khao Tom Koong

  5. You need 1/4 tsp of salt.
  6. It's 1/2 tsp of black pepper.

Thai Rice soup with Prawns / Khao Tom Koong instructions

  1. Today i use rice cooker,but normally can do on stove top in same direction ,add water or stock you have ,half close lid ,let it cook until rice more soft ,water dissolve.
  2. Open lid,seasoning.
  3. Add prawns ,dont stir,let it cook about 2 - 3 mins ,add chopped spring onion ,crispy garlic on top ..you can add more prawns for second bowl too..
  4. Enjoy as late night supper or as breakfast like Thai ways :).
