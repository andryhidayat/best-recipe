Ingredients of Mix Lentils Soup with Fries

  1. You need 1/2 cup of pigeon peas.
  2. Prepare 1/2 cup of split red lentil.
  3. It's 1/4 cup of split green gram.
  4. You need 2 1/2 glass of water.
  5. You need 1 tsp of oil.
  6. You need 1/2 tsp of Ginger garlic paste.
  7. Prepare 1/2 tsp of turmeric powder.
  8. Prepare 1/2 tsp of black pepper powder.
  9. You need 2 tsp of fresh cream or butter.
  10. Prepare To taste of salt.

Mix Lentils Soup with Fries instructions

  1. Take a cooker add lentils,turmeric powder,salt and water and cook it until 4-5 whistle comes..
  2. Now let it be for little bit cool down and mash it well..
  3. Heat oil in a pan add ginger-garlic paste and after 10 seconds add cooked lentils mix and let boil for once now add black pepper and fresh cream and mix it nicely and cook for 2 minutes..
  4. Soup is ready to serve with crispy fries..
