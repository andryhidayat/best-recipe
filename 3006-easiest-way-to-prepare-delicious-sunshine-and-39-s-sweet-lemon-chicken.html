Ingredients of sunshine's sweet lemon chicken

  1. Prepare 1 of 2 to 3 lb chicken breast.
  2. It's 3 tbsp of all purpose flour.
  3. It's 1/2 tsp of salt.
  4. Prepare 4 tbsp of vegetable oil divided.
  5. It's 1 of 12.oz frozen lemonade concentrated thawed and undiluted.
  6. Prepare 1/2 cup of water.
  7. You need 1/4 cup of light brown sugar.
  8. You need 3 tbsp of ketchup.
  9. You need 2 tsp of white vinegar.
  10. It's 2 tbsp of cornstarch.
  11. It's 2 tbsp of water.

sunshine's sweet lemon chicken step by step

  1. combine flour and salt ,dredge chicken in flour mixture,... heat 2 T, oil in a large skillet add chicken and cook over medium heat Until lightly brown turning once. till all pices are done add the rest of oil as needed..
  2. combined. Lemonade ,a half a cup of water, brown sugar, ketchup ,and the vinegar ...mix well, pour mixture over chicken in skillet, cover and cook for 25 minutes, stirring occasionally..
  3. remove the chicken from the skillet place and chicken on a serving platter cover with foil keeping warm.
  4. now combine the cornstarch and the two tablespoons of water in a small bowl and mix well. gradually add the cornstarch mix to the juice stirring constantly in the skillet , stirring until smooth and thickened, spoon over chicken pieces and serve, careful not to burn yourself it's thick and sticky.
  5. enjoy !!!!!!.
