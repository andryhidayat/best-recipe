Ingredients of Greek Lentil soup

  1. You need 300 g of brown lentils.
  2. You need 2 tbsp of olive oil.
  3. It's 2 of onions.
  4. It's 2 cloves of garlic.
  5. Prepare 1 can of chopped tomatoes.
  6. It's 4 cans of water.
  7. Prepare 2 of bay leafs.
  8. It's 1/4 tsp of sugar.
  9. Prepare 1/2 tsp of oregano.
  10. You need of Kalamata olives.
  11. You need of Wine vinegar.

Greek Lentil soup step by step

  1. Boil the lentils for 7min, drain and discard the water.
  2. Chop onions and garlic.
  3. Fry onions, then garlic.
  4. Add lentils, mix with oil and onions for 1min.
  5. Add can of tomatoes and 2 cans of water.
  6. Add sugar, oregano and bay leafs.
  7. Simmer for 23min.
  8. Server with dash of vinegar and olives.
