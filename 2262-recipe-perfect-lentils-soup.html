Lentils soup step by step

  1. Fry onion, garlic, chopped tomatoes and carrots in 3 tbsp of oil, let them simmer for a while.
  2. Add laurel leaf, salt, black pepper, powder of coriander, paprika and RAS EL HANOUT.
  3. Let them simmer for a while then add tomato paste, lentils and hot water.
  4. Cook it for a while, blend it when it gets ready, serve it with chopped coriander and bit of olive oil.
