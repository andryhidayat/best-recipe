  5. You need 4 of cloves of peeled/diced garlic.

  7. You need 1/2 cup of shredded pepper jack cheese.
  8. Prepare 1/4 cup of BBQ sauce.

Jalapeño popper rice step by step

  1. Prepare rice (this part takes the longest time).
  2. Cook bacon until it starts to brown..
  3. Add sausage to bacon and brown.
  4. Add diced jalapeños, onion and garlic to bacon and sausage. Cook for 5 minutes on medium heat..
  5. Add BBQ sauce to pan stir in.
  6. Divide prepared rice into two bowls. Cover rice with cheese..
  7. Spoon sausage/bacon/jalapeno mixture over rice..
  8. Serve with more BBQ sauce.
