Ingredients of Sloppy Joe Turnovers

  1. You need 1 lb of ground beef.
  2. It's 1/2 tsp of onion salt.
  3. It's 1/2 cup of ketchup.
  4. Prepare 1/4 cup of sour cream.
  5. You need 1/2 tsp of salt.
  6. Prepare 1/4 tsp of garlic powder.
  7. You need 12 oz of canned refrigerated flaky biscuits.
  8. It's 2 tbsp of butter, melted.

Sloppy Joe Turnovers step by step

  1. Cook ground beef with onion salt in large skillet, stirring continually, until ground beef is browned & crumbly. Drain in colander..
  2. Return ground beef mixture to skillet. Stir in ketchup, sour cream, salt, & Garlic powder..
  3. Preheat oven to 375 degrees..
  4. Pat or roll each biscuit into a 4 inch square on a lightly floured surface. Spoon 1/4 cup of the ground beef mixture in the center of each biscuit square. Fold over the corner of each square to form a triangle. Seal edges with a fork..
  5. Arrange turnovers on ungreased cookie sheet. Cut three 1/2 inch slots in top of each turnover. Brush tops with melted butter..
  6. Bake until golden brown, about 15-20 minutes. Let cool for 5 minutes. Serve..
