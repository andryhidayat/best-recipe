Banana Bread Recipe taken from @happywivescooking Tried and tested by @hrns_cuisine step by step

  1. Sift dry ingredients (flour, bicarbonate of soda and baking powder) together..
  2. In a separate bowl, mix the bananas, oil and eggs together..
  3. Add sugar.
  4. Make a well in the dry ingredients, pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and mix well..
  5. Grease a bread pan and pour the mixture into it..
  6. Bake at 160 degrees C for 30-45 minutes..
