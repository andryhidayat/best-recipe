Ingredients of Mutton Afghani recipe

Mutton Afghani recipe step by step

  1. Take a pressure cooker add mutton,ginger garlic paste,salt 1/3 tsp and 5-6 tsp water....when 1 whistle come slow the gas flame and cook for 10 to 15 minutes......
  2. Now check the mutton if mutton has 90% cooked and have any extra water then dry it...after that take a mixer jar put green chili,onion, coriander leaves,ginger garlic and add little bit of water make a paste..
  3. Now paste is ready transfer in a bowl and again take same jar add cream,curd and milk and grind it once and transfer in a same bowl now add mutton in the paste..
  4. Add chaat masala,kasuri methi salt,garam masala,lemon juice,2 tsp of vegetable mix everything very well.keep aside for 30 minutes..
  5. After 30 minute, Take a pan add 1 tsp oil,gas should be high flame,once oil hot put mutton in the pan and cooked for 5 minutes.mutton has already cooked we want only brown spot on the mutton..
  6. Again take a big pan add 3 tsp of oil when oil is hot add gravy and cover the pan and cook for 5 minutes on high flame..after 5 minutes add red chilly powder and again cover the pan and cook per 10 minutes on slow flame and stir spoon between....now 10 minutes has complete add mutton mix if well add 5-6 tsp of water mix it and again cover the pan cook for 10 minutes on slow flame.....
  7. Now 10 minutes complete this time gas flame off..mutton is completely cooked...take a charcoal burn it and take a small steel bowl or aluminium foil put inside the pan add 1 clove and green Cardemom and put little bit of desi ghee and cove the pan at least for 10 minutes...for smoky and grilled flavour enhance the taste of Mutton Afghani..
  8. Now Mutton Afghani is ready to serve....serve with Naan,Shermal,pratha or rice....thanks.
