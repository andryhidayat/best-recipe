Ingredients of Lemon Curd with Poppy Seeds

  1. Prepare 4 of lemons, juiced (about 1/2 cup juice).
  2. Prepare 2 of eggs.
  3. It's 3 of egg yolks.
  4. You need 2 Tbsp of milk.
  5. It's 75 g of sugar (1/3 cup).
  6. You need 1/4 tsp of salt.
  7. You need 1/2 Tbsp of Poppy seeds.
  8. It's 85 g of unsalted butter.

Lemon Curd with Poppy Seeds step by step

  1. Zest one of the lemons. Juice all four of the lemons so you get about 1/2 cup lemon juice (120 ml).
  2. Whisk together the eggs, egg yolks, milk, sugar, and poppy seeds. Add the lemon juice and zest and mix..
  3. Cut butter in little cubes, add to the egg mixture..
  4. Put mixture in a non-reactive pot and heat on medium low..
  5. Stirring constantly, cook until the mixture is thick enough to coat the back of a spoon. Do not let it boil or eggs will curdle!.
  6. Put in a jar, let cool and store in the fridge..
