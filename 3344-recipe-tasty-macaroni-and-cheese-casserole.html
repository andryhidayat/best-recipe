Macaroni and cheese casserole instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F.
  2. Boil macaroni and while that is going on mix the milk with the parsley , italian seasoning salt and pepper.... Also add the finely chopped an sauted onions and garlic.
  3. Then mix already boiled macaroni with the two and a half cup of mayonaise ......stir.
  4. Then occasionaly mix in the milk and butter.
  5. Put in half of the amount of mixed macaroni and then put in a layer of cheese .... Just enough to cover the pan.
  6. Then put in the other half and mix in the parmesean and cheddar cheese in.
  7. Put in oven and wait 35 mins.
  8. Wait to cool.
  9. Slice and serve.
