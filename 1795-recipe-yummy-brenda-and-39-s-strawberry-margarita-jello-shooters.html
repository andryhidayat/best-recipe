Brenda's Strawberry Margarita Jello Shooters step by step

  1. Strawberry Prep: Cut bottoms of strawberries flat to get them to stand upright..
  2. Make sure not to cut to much as to cause a hole in the bottom..
  3. Use a huller or apple corer to scoop out centers.
  4. Pat centers dry. May use mini muffin tins to keep strawberries upright if needed..
  5. Margarita Mix: Boil 1 cup hot water in saucepan, then add jello powder and whisk until completely dissolved..
  6. Combine tequila and Cointreau in cocktail shaker filled with ice. (or put in fridge to chill first).
  7. Add liquor mixture to 1/4 cup cold water and stir to combine.
  8. Add cold mixture to hot jello mixture..
  9. Pour final mixture into strawberries and chill overnight..
  10. If you like salt, dip finger in wader or use wet brush to wet edge of strawberries, dip and rotate in salt to rim..
  11. May garnish with lime pieces to finish..
