Ingredients of The best banana bread ever

  1. You need 1 cup of melted butter.
  2. You need 1 3/4 cup of dark brown sugar.
  3. You need 4 of eggs.
  4. Prepare 3 cup of flour.
  5. You need 1 cup of milk.
  6. It's 3/4 tsp of salt.
  7. You need 3 tsp of baking soda.
  8. It's 4 cup of mashed bananas (about 8 bananas ).
  9. Prepare 1 tbsp of vanilla.
  10. Prepare 1 1/2 cup of chopped walnuts (opional).

The best banana bread ever step by step

  1. preheat oven to 350.
  2. mix butter , brown sugar , and eggs in large bowl.
  3. add flour , salt , baking soda and milk . mix well.
  4. add vanilla and bananas then mix well.
  5. if adding walnuts stir them in.
  6. spray pans with nonstick cooking spray . Divide into 3 pans and bake for 55 to 60 minutes.
