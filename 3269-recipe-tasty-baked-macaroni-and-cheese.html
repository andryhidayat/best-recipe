Ingredients of Baked Macaroni and Cheese

  1. It's of ingredients.
  2. You need 4 cup of uncooked elbow macaroni.
  3. It's 3/4 cup of onion, finely diced (I cut this back to 1/2 cup).
  4. Prepare 3/4 cup of butter, divided.
  5. It's 6 tbsp of all-purpose flour.
  6. You need 4 1/2 cup of milk.
  7. It's 4 cup of (16 ounces) shredded sharp cheddar cheese. (You can substitute Velvetta if you'd like).
  8. You need 1 tsp of dry mustard.
  9. It's 1/4 tsp of ground black pepper.
  10. You need 1 tsp of salt.
  11. Prepare 3/4 cup of dry bread crumbs (I use panko).

Baked Macaroni and Cheese step by step

  1. On stovetop, Cook macaroni according to package directions to al dente. ALSO, preheat oven to 350Â°F..
  2. Meanwhile, in sauce pan, saute onion in 1/2 cup butter. Once butter is melted, wisk in flour. Cook on low heat until a light golden brown, making a roux. (I omitted the onion completely this time, just to experiment).
  3. Gradually add the milk, bring to a low boil. Stir constantly until thickened. About 5 minutes on a med/low..
  4. Add dry mustard, salt, pepper and cheese, stir until cheese is melted, and sauce is smooth. Mix cooked macaroni in..
  5. In a greased 9x13 inch pan, pour the macaroni and cheese in. In separate bowl, mix bread crumbs with remaining 1/4 cup melted butter. Sprinkle evenly on top. Place uncovered in oven for 30-35 minutes, or until heated through and bubbly..
  6. I finished it off by placing under broiler for a couple of minutes to lightly brown the panko bread crumbs. WATCH CAREFULLY! Or you'll have burned bread crumbs!.
