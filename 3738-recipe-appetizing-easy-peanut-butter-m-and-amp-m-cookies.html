  3. You need 1 tsp of Vanilla.
  4. You need 1 of Egg.
  5. You need 1/2 of M&Ms.

Easy peanut butter m&m cookies instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350Â°F.
  2. Mix sugar, Egg, vanilla, peanut Butter and sugar in a bowl..
  3. roll into medium size balls and place on greased or ungreased cookie sheet (whichever you prefer).
  4. take a fork and dip it into sugar and make a criss cross pattern by pressing down slightly on the cookies ..
  5. place as many M&Ms you would like on each cookie. Five seemed to be the right amount for me.
  6. place in preheated over about 7 mins. longer if you prefer them darker and not as soft..
  7. once they are done leave them on cookie sheet to cool for atleast 2mins. this is crucial if you want them to stay together.
  8. after that enjoy them with a tall glass of milk!.
