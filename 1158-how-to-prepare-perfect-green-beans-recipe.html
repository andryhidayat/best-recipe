Green Beans Recipe instructions

  1. Wash the beans and remove the hard stem from it from one side. Put a cut on the other side and divide the long beans into two equal parts..
  2. In the pan add oil, garlic,sesame seeds saute for a few minutes..
  3. Now add the beans to it. Cover the pan with a lid and steam cook the beans for 4-6 minutes, till they turn soft. Turn off the heat and keep the bowl of beans aside..
  4. Saute for 1-2 minutes. Turn off the flame. Sesame Green Bean Salad is ready. For taste add black pepper and lemon juice..
