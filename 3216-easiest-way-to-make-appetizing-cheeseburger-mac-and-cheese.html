Ingredients of Cheeseburger Mac And Cheese

  1. Prepare 1 lb of Lean ground beef.
  2. Prepare 1/4 box of Velveeta process cheese, cubed.
  3. It's 2 cups of Milk or half and half, divided.
  4. Prepare 1/4 tsp of Cayenne pepper.
  5. Prepare 1 of Salt and pepper.
  6. You need 1 of Onion and garlic powders, I used Everglades seasoning.
  7. Prepare 1 lb of Pasta of choice, I used bow ties.
  8. Prepare 1 tbsp of AP flour.
  9. You need 1 cup of Panko bread crumbs mixed with 2 tablespoons oil and salt and pepper.

Cheeseburger Mac And Cheese instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375.
  2. Melt cubed velveeta and 1 cup milk or half and half in microwave til smooth..
  3. Par-boil pasta for about 3 minutes, drain..
  4. Brown ground beef and season. Once browned stir in flour til absorbed. Splash in last cup of milk and stir to a boil and it thickens. Turn off heat. Stir cheese sauce into beef mixture. Add pasta and stir..
  5. Poor into sprayed dish and cover with foil. Bake about 20 minutes. Remove foil and add bread crumbs. Crank heat to 425 til browned. Enjoy.
