Turkey Chili Stuffed Potatoes step by step

  1. In a large skillet, cook onion and garlic for 3-4 minutes..
  2. Add ground turkey, cook, breaking up while it browns..
  3. When turkey is almost cooked through, add spices and diced tomatoes..
  4. Let finish cooking and simmer for 15 minutes..
  5. Open up the potatoes lengthwise, divide chili among the potatoes. Top each with ¼ C. of cheese and a few dashes of hair sauce, if using..
