Chicken and rice stoup step by step

  1. Get water hot add chicken.
  2. After 4 minutes add vegetables and spices and butter.
  3. After 15 minutes take chicken from pot remove skin and put back in becareful not to burn yourself.
  4. Add your rice keep stirring occasionally to keep rice from sticking.
  5. After rice is done mix quarter cup water and quarter cup of cornstarch add to water rice to thicken.
  6. After it thickens let sit for 5 minutes covered.
  7. Hope you enjoy.
