Game Day GUACAMOLE! step by step

  1. cut avocados in half remove pit & scoop out inside & put in bowl.
  2. dice onion & remove seeds from peppers & mince & put both in bowl.
  3. add salt...olive oil & juice from lime into the bowl.
  4. chop up cilantro & put into bowl.
  5. mash & mix until desired consistency is reached.
  6. serve with chips & enjoy!.
