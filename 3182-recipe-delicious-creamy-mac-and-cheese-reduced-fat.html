Ingredients of Creamy Mac And Cheese (Reduced Fat)

  1. Prepare 1 Cup of cheddar cheese.
  2. It's 1 Box of Elbow Macaroni.
  3. You need 1 Can of Lite Table Cream.
  4. Prepare 1 Can of 2% Evaporated Milk.
  5. Prepare 16 of Velveeta Slices (One Pack).
  6. Prepare 1/2 Cup of Country Crock (Churn Style).
  7. Prepare 1/2 Cup of Shredded Parmesan.

Creamy Mac And Cheese (Reduced Fat) instructions

  1. Bring 6 cups of water to a boil, add elbow macaroni, stir occasionally until al dente (about 8-10 at med temp). Drain, set aside..
  2. In a large nonstick pan heat lite table cream, 2% evaporated milk, and country crock. Once heated start adding Velveeta slices throughout and continue stirring until fully mixed..
  3. Add 1/2 cup Parmesan until mixed. Begin adding pasta and stir until fully covered. Add shredded cheddar cheese and continue stirring for another minute..
  4. Transfer to container. Refrigerate leftovers for up to four days..
