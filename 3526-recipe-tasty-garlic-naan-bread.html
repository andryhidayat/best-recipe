Garlic naan bread instructions

  1. Mix the sugar,yeast and Luke water together..
  2. In a pan,gently melt the butter and the grated garlic. Remove from fire and let it cool..
  3. Mix the dry ingredients, flour,salt,milk,pour whisked egg,proofed yeast and little water to make a soft dough. knead it well for about 5mins.
  4. Cover with a lid and let it rise to double it size.Knead again an cut into small pieces/balls.
  5. Flour a clean surface and roll each ball into a tin flat layer.
  6. Heat your non stick pan on very high heat and gently place the rolled pieces,you will note some bubbles on top the flip it to the other side..
  7. You can enjoy it with any sauce of your choice!!!.
