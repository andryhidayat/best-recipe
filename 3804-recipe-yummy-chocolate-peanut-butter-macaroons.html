Ingredients of Chocolate peanut butter macaroons

  1. Prepare 2 cup of sugar.
  2. You need 1/2 cup of unsalted butter.
  3. You need 1/2 cup of milk.
  4. It's 1 tsp of vanilla.
  5. Prepare 1/2 cup of peanut butter.
  6. You need 3 cup of quick cooking oats.
  7. Prepare 1 cup of semi sweet coconut (shredded).
  8. You need 2 tbsp of cocoa powder.
  9. It's 1/8 tsp of salt.
  10. Prepare 1/4 cup of milk chocolate chips.

Chocolate peanut butter macaroons step by step

  1. In medium sized bowl combine oats coconut cocoa salt and mix together and set aside.
  2. In saucepan combine sugar butter milk vanilla peanut butter stir together bring to a boil for at least a minute then pour in to the bowl with the oats ...stir together and drop by tbs on wax paper let set and enjoy !.
