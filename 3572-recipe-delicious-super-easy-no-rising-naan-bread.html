Super Easy, No-Rising Naan Bread step by step

  1. Put the dry ingredients in a bowl and loosely combine. Add the yogurt and vegetable oil. Knead by hand for about 5 minutes..
  2. Divide the dough in half and stretch it flat..
  3. Grease the frying pan lightly with oil (amount not listed in the ingredients), and add the naan-shaped dough..
  4. Fry over low-medium heat until nicely browned, flip and brown the other side (for a total of 10-15 minutes)..
