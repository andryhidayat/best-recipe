So Easy and So Tasty Baked Spaghetti step by step

  1. In a large pot, cook spaghetti according to package instructions..
  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine cheeses and other ingredients until smooth and mixed thoroughly..
  3. Add spaghetti to sauce/cheese mix until spaghetti is completely coated..
  4. Pour into medium greased casserole dish and bake at 350 degrees for 20 minutes covered..
  5. Uncover and bake another 10 minutes until cheese is golden brown..
  6. Remove from oven and let cool for 5-7 minutes and serve and ENJOY!!.
